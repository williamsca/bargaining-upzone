 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing on the proposed FY20 advertised real estate tax rate of $1.0875 per $100 of assessed value, a $0.0275 increase from the current city adopted rate of $1.06. The difference between the equalized tax rate and the proposed tax rate would be $0.041 per $100, or 3.94 percent.  This difference will be known as the "effective tax rate increase."  Individual property taxes may, however, increase at a percentage greater than or less than the above percentage. (Tax Rate to be set on May 8, 2019.)

 
No Council action.

No Council action.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $260,875 for the City’s required local contribution into the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA) Capital Fund.

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $260,875 for the City’s required local contribution into WMATA’s Capital Fund.
