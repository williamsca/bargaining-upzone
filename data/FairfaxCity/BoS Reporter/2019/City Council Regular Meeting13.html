                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         July 23, 2019 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember DeMarco).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.  None.
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Introduction of an appropriation resolution in the amount of $502,425.33 to fund the replacement of self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) for the Fire Department.

 
Approved, unanimously, consent agenda items #7a, which carried unanimously by voice vote. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending and reenacting Chapter 6, Article III, Division 2, of the Code of the City of Fairfax Virginia, relating to the licensing of dogs.

 
Approved, unanimously an ordinance amending and reenacting Chapter 6, Article III, Division 2, Section 6-81 and 6-82 of the code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia relating to the licensing of dogs. (Councilmember Passey was absent)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $3,800,000.00 to fully fund the Fire Station No. 33 Replacement Project.

 
Approved, unanimously to approve the supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $3,800,000.00 to fully fund the fire station No. 33 replacement project. (Councilmember Passey was absent)
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of an award of construction contract to Whitener & Jackson, Inc. in the amount of for construction of the Fire Station No. 33 Replacement Project.

 

Approved, unanimously that the City Council award a contract to Whitener & Jackson, Inc. in the amount of $11,126,438.00 for the construction of the fire station No. 33 replacement project and authorize the City Manager to execute the contract provided that the contract and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney. (Councilmember Passey was absent)
 
 

b.

Consideration of the Bikeshare Feasibility Study Report recommendations.

 
Approved, 4-1, Councilmember Yi opposed, to accept the final report of the Fairfax-Mason-Vienna Bikeshare Feasibility study. He further moves to endorse the recommendations of the study as guidance for potential future implementation. 
 
 
 
 10.  Approval of minutes.

a.

June 25, 2019 regular meeting.

 
Approved, unanimously the minutes of the June 25th 2019 regular meeting as amended by Councilmember Yi.  (Councilmember Passey was absent)
 
 
 
 11.  Recess the regular meeting to go into a work session.

a.

Discussion of the Old Lee Highway Multimodal Improvement Design and Utility Options.

 
 
 13.  Reconvene the regular meeting.
 
 14.  Council Comments.
 
 15.  Closed meeting to discuss personnel matters, acquisition of property for public purpose and consultation with legal counsel regarding possible litigation.
 
 16.  Adjourn the Regular Meeting.
