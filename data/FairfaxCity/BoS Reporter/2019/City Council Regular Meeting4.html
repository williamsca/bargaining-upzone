a.

Public hearing on the City Manager’s proposed FY20 budget. (Budget Adoption on May 8, 2019)

 

No Council action was taken.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation in the amount of $3,741,923 for the Chain Bridge Road Sidewalk Project.

 

Approved 4-2, a supplemental appropriation in the amount of $3,741,923 for the transfer of C&I monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund to fund construction of the Chain Bridge Road Sidewalk Project. (Councilmembers Passey and Yi opposed.)
 
 

9.

Items not requiring a public hearing.

 

a.

Consideration of the maximum real estate tax rate for fiscal year 2019-20 to be advertised. (This action sets the maximum real estate tax rate that can be adopted as part of the fiscal year 2019-20 budget. This  action does not set the actual real estate tax rate for fiscal year 2019-20.)

 

Approved 4-2, to advertise a proposed 2019 real estate tax rate of $1.0875 per $100 of assessed value. (Councilmembers Lim and Yi opposed.)
 
 

b.

Consideration of an award of contract to Sagres Construction in an amount not to exceed $4,467,755 for the Chain Bridge Road Sidewalk Project.

 

Approved 4-2, to award a contract to Sagres Construction in an amount not to exceed $4,467,755 for the Chain Bridge Road Sidewalk Project contingent on VDOT authorization to award, provided that the contract and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney. (Councilmembers Passey and Yi opposed.)
