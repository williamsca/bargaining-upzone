                         October 29, 2019 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.

 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration to authorize the City to sell two (2) cemetery sites to a non-city resident.

 

b.

Introduction of an appropriation resolution in the maximum amount of $12,000 to Fair Lakes Youth Association ($7,500), Yellow Ribbons United ($2,000) and The Josh Anderson Foundation ($2,500).

 

c.

Introduction of an appropriation resolution in the amount of $99,910 to fund an Organizational and Staffing Analysis.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda 7a, to approve the suggested motion in the staff report, and for agenda item 7b and 7c, to waive the first reading and set the public hearing for November 12, 2019.
(Councilmember Yi was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Tellez Fairfax LLC, applicant, by Dario Davies, agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5.C to allow drive-through windows/facilities as an accessory use to a restaurant in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 10880 Fairfax Blvd and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-02-048-B.

 
Approved 4-1 to move that the City Council approve the request of Tellez Fairfax LLC, applicant, by Dario Davies, agent for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5.C to drive-through windows/facilities as an accessory use to a restaurant in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 10880 Fairfax Blvd and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-02-048-B, subject to the following conditions:
  1. Operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and plans, subject to minor modification by the Zoning Administrator.
  2. Applicant shall remove, modify or replace the existing non-conforming pylon sign. Any modification or replacement shall reduce the extent of the nonconformity. In accordance with the provisions of section 7.5.3 and meet the description requirement for monument signs in the Zoning Ordinance.
(Councilmember Yi was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 6 (Animals) of the Code of City of Fairfax, Virginia, to add thereto a new Article VI, pertaining to pet shops.

 
Approved, unanimously by vote to move to approve an ordinance amending Chapter 6 pf the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to add thereto a new article VI pertaining to pet shops.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an initial ordinance proposing to grant a non-exclusive franchise to occupy and use the public rights-of-way, streets, highways, parks and other public places of City for the construction, maintenance and operation wherein of a system of pipes, mains, valves, manholes necessary or convenient for the transmission, distribution and sale of natural gas within the corporate limits of the City, and authorizing the advertisement and receipt of bids for the franchise.

 
Approved, unanimously move to enact an initial ordinance proposing to grant a non-exclusive franchise to occupy and use the public right-of-way of the city for the purpose of construction, maintenance and operation of a system of pipes, mains, valves, manholes, connections, meters and other equipment and appliances necessary or convenient for the transmission, distribution and sale of natural gas within the corporate limits of the city as the same now exist or may hereafter be extended or altered, and establishing procedures for the advertisement and solicitation of bids for such franchise.

(Councilmember Yi was absent.)
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

Consideration of a resolution providing for the issuance and sale of two series of general obligation refunding bonds of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, providing for the form, details and payment thereof, providing for the refunding of a portion of the city’s outstanding general obligation refunding bonds, series 2012B, and authorizing the execution and delivery of certain documents, all for the purpose to realize debt service savings.

 
Approved, unanimously to move that the City Council adopt a resolution providing for the issuance and sale of two series of general obligation refunding bonds of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, providing for the form, details and payment thereof, providing for the refunding of a portion of the City's outstanding general obligation refunding bonds, series 2012B, and authorizing the execution and delivery of certain documents in connection therewith.
(Councilmember Yi was absent)

Approved, unanimously to move that the City Council adopt a resolution providing for the issuance and sale of a general obligation refunding bond of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, providing for the form, details and payment thereof, providing for the refunding of a portion of the City's outstanding general obligation refunding bonds, series 2010, and authorizing the execution and delivery of certain documents in connection therewith.
