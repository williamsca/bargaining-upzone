                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 11, 2019 
 
   Closed meeting to conduct interviews for Boards and Commissions and discuss appointments to Boards and Commissions. 6:00 p.m.
 
   REGULAR MEETING  7:00 P.M.

1.

Call the regular meeting to order.

 

2.

Invocation (Councilmember DeMarco).

 

3.

Pledge of Allegiance.

 

4.

Presentations and Proclamations.

 

a.

Presentation of a proclamation acknowledging June as LGBTQ+ Pride Month.

 

5.

Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.

 

6.

Adoption of the agenda.

 

7.

Consent agenda.

 

a.

Consideration of a resolution adopting the proposed right-of-way (ROW) license and permit process for wireless carrier small-cell antenna placements.

 

b.

Consideration of an award of a five-year contract in the amount of $464,500 to Robinson, Farmer, Cox Associates for external audit services for FY2019 - FY2023 with five optional one-year renewals.

 

c.

Introduction of an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease for a term beginning July 1, 2019 and ending June 30, 2022 by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia, and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc., for approximately 7,364 square feet of space in the former Green Acres Elementary School, located at 4401 Sideburn Road.

 

d.

Introduction of an ordinance amending Chapter 90, Article II, Division 3 of the City Code relating to tax relief program exemptions for the elderly and disabled.

 

Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items. (Councilmember Yi recused himself from #a and Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

8.

Public hearings.

 

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $2,541,862 for FY 2019 nondiscretionary expenditures and City Council approved expenditures for the General Fund, Capital Fund, Sherwood Legacy Fund and Transit Fund.

 

Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $2,541,862 for FY 2019 nondiscretionary expenditures and City Council approved expenditures for the General Fund, Capital Fund, Sherwood Legacy Fund and Transit Fund. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $250,596 for Fiscal Year 2018-2019 Federal, State and other grant expenditures.

 

Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $250,596 for FY 2018-2019 Federal, State and other grant expenditures. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on appropriation resolutions for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater, Cable, Old Town, and Transportation tax funds for the FY2020 budget year.

 

Approved, unanimously, the appropriation resolutions for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater, Cable, Old Town, and Transportation Tax Funds for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2019. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $4,500,000 for the Northfax Storm Drainage Project (VDOT UPC 16632).

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation in the amount of $4,500,000 and to transfer C&I monies $2,250,000 from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund to fund construction of the Northfax Intersection Improvement Project. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

9.

Items not requiring a public hearing.  None.
