 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of a resolution setting the allocation percentage for Personal Property Tax Relief (PPTR) in the City of Fairfax for the 2019 Tax Year at 47.40%.

 

Approved, unanimously, a resolution setting the allocation percentage for Personal Property Tax Relief for tax year 2019.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.    None.
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of the establishment of a dockless shared mobility pilot program in the City of Fairfax.

 

Approved, unanimously, to authorize a demonstration project (pilot program) for shared mobility devices on the terms and conditions outlined by the staff in the staff report and as presented at the meeting and adopt the pilot program policies and procedures permitting and regulating the use of shared mobility devices in and about the City’s public rights of way, subject to adjustments deemed necessary or appropriate by staff, in consultation with the City Attorney.

May 7, 2019 Special Meeting.

 
 
 11.  Closed meeting to discuss appointments to boards and commissions, acquisition of real property for public purpose and for consultation with legal counsel for litigation and legal advice.
 
 12.  Actions out of Closed Meeting/Appointments to Boards and Commissions.
  • Appointed Soledad Portilla to the Economic Development Authority for a four-year term extending through May 14, 2023 and conditional upon the appointee satisfying all legal requirements for filling/assuming the position, including but not limited to, completing the mandatory statement of economic interests and filing it with the City Clerk, within five (5) business days of the appointment. If the appointee fails or refuses to timely complete the statement of economic interests, the appointment will automatically be rescinded and the position will be filled again according to City Council procedures.
  • Appointed Alexandria Gardner to the Human Services Committee for the remainder of a three-year term extending through July 23, 2020, with an extension of a three-year term extending through July 23, 2023.
  • Reappointed Caryn Perrelli Trivett to the Personnel Advisory Board for a three-year term extending through June 11, 2022.
  • Reappointed Jamil Ahmed to The Retirement Plan Administrative Committee for a three-year term extending through June 12, 2022.
  • Approved, unanimously, to authorize the purchase by the City of certain real property comprising approximately 4.24 acres, with improvements, located within the City, and owned by the County of Fairfax, identified as tax map nos. 57-3-02-013 and 57-3-02-012, and having an address of 10635 and 10637 West Drive, Fairfax, Virginia and (1) authorize and direct the staff to finalize a purchase and sale agreement on the terms discussed in closed meeting; and (2) authorize the City Manager and/or the Mayor, with the concurrence of the City Attorney, to execute a final purchase and sale agreement with the County of Fairfax for the West Drive property, and for the City to undertake its obligations under such agreement.
