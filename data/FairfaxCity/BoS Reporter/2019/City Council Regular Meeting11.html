Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease for a term beginning July 1, 2019 and ending June 30, 2022 by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia, and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc., for approximately 7,364 square feet of space in the former Green Acres Elementary School, located at 4401 Sideburn Road.

 
Approved, unanimously,  an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc. for premises located at 4401 Sideburn Road (a portion of the former Green Acres Elementary School).
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 90, Article II, Division 3 of the City Code relating to tax relief program exemptions for the elderly and disabled.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 90, Article II, Division 3 of the city code relating to tax relief program exemptions for the elderly and disabled.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on proposed amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 (Zoning) to amend portions of articles and sections contained therein pertaining to Telecommunications Facilities and the types and standards associated therewith.

 
Approved, unanimously with one recusal, an ordinance amending Chapter 110 (Zoning) to amend portions of articles and sections contained therein pertaining to Telecommunications Facilities and the types and standards associated therewith.
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of PM Pediatrics Management Group LLC., applicant, by Molly Novotny, agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.B to allow a Medical Care Facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District on the premises known as 11056 Lee Hwy and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-02-036.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of PM Pediatrics Management Group LLC., applicant, by Molly Novotny, agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.B to allow a Medical Care Facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District on the premises known as 11056 Lee Hwy and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-02-036. Subject to the following conditions:

1)Operation of this establishment shall be consistent with application, statement of support, and plans. Zoning Administrator shall be enabled to approve minor changes to the time schedule. 

2)All local, state, and federal licenses and permits shall be maintained and kept current.
