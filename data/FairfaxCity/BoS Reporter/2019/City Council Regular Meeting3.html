a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Fairfax Veterinary Hospital, applicant, by John Ranivand, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.B to allow an animal care facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District on the premises known as 10396 Willard Way and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-2-20-004D.

 
Approved, unanimously, the request of Fairfax Veterinary Hospital, applicant, by John Ranivand, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.b to allow an animal care facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District on the premises known as 10396 Willard Way and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-2-20-004d, subject to the following conditions:  
  1. Operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and plans.
  2. The applicant shall limit the hours of operation to seven days a week, 7:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m.
  3. The applicant shall incorporate sound mitigating measures into the design as shown on the submitted plans and details, for the proposed veterinarian hospital, to prevent noise levels, if any, from creating any adverse impact on adjacent commercial establishments or nearby residential properties.
  4. The applicant shall ensure all activities associated with the care and treatment of animals shall occur within the building.
  5. The applicant shall provide for proper disposal of medical waste and euthanized animals as required by local, state and federal regulations. All storage of waste shall be inside the facility.
  6. The applicant shall monitor the shopping center premises to ensure that no animal waste accumulation occurs.
  7. All local, state and federal licenses and permits shall be maintained and kept current.
  8. The Special Use Permit shall be valid for a period of two years. Prior to expiration, the applicant shall apply for renewal of the Special Use Permit application or it shall be deemed null and void.
(Councilmember Lim was absent.)
