Public hearings.

 

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $81,615.49 to fund the replacement of the audiovisual equipment in Stacy C. Sherwood Community Center.

 

Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $81,615.49 to fund the replacement of the audiovisual system at Stacy C. Sherwood Community Center with funding from the Sherwood Legacy account.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article IV of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to repeal and reenact Division 3 (pertaining to residential parking permits), and to add a new Division 4 (establishing a Green Acres Center Permit Parking District).

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article IV of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to repeal and reenact Division 3 (pertaining to Residential Parking Permits), and to add a new Division 4 (establishing a Green Acres Center Parking Permit District) with additional language to Section 98-215(a) as presented by the City Attorney and striking/adding suggested language Section 98-216(a) as presented by staff.

No Council action taken.
 
 

b.

Approved, unanimously, to authorize Phase II of preliminary engineering services for an amount not to exceed $815,731 for the Old Lee Highway Multimodal Improvements Project under the existing contract with Rinker Design Associates, P.C. for this project.
