 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Hua Jin, applicant, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B to allow for an indoor recreation use (Jolly Yolly Kids) in the CR Commercial Retail zoning district and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 11001 Lee Hwy, Fairfax, VA 22030 and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-02-128.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Hua Jin, applicant, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.b for an indoor recreation use (Jolly Yolly Kids) in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 11001 Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-02-128, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The business shall operate in accordance with the statement of justification and floor plans submitted with the special use permit application, except that interior modifications may be allowed.
  2. The hours of operation shall be Monday through Sunday 8:00 am to 11:00 pm.
  3. Noise levels shall comply with operational performance standards for noise in the Zoning Ordinance for commercial uses.
  4. All activities shall occur indoors and be limited to the leased areas identified on the approved floor plan.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on the adoption of the City of Fairfax 2035 Comprehensive Plan.

 

Approved, unanimously, that the City Council adopt the resolution approving and adopting the City of Fairfax 2035 Comprehensive Plan in the draft dated February 6, 2019 and further accepting the City of Fairfax 2035 Comprehensive Plan Implementation Guide as a resource in implementing the recommendations of the comprehensive plan.
