 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from 9700 Fairfax Blvd LLC, applicant, by Robert D. Brant, agent/attorney, for consideration of a Zoning Map Amendment (Rezoning) pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.4 from IH, Industrial Heavy and CR, Commercial Retail to CR, Commercial Retail while retaining the Architectural Control Overlay District (ACOD), with proffers; a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 3.3 (Allowed Uses) for fuel stations in CR, Commercial Retail District; a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.7 from the requirement that on-site utilities are required to be located underground per Section 4.11 (Underground Utilities), a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.7 to modify the required 10-foot required side (interior yard) per Section 3.6.2 (Nonresidential Districts), and a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.7 to modify the requirement for street trees within 15-feet of the back of curb per Section 4.5.6.B.1 (Street Trees); and for a Certificate of Appropriateness pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.5 for architecture and landscaping, to allow the development of a grocery store and fuel stations on the premises known as 9700 Fairfax Boulevard and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 48-3-09-056. (Wawa)

 
Deferred (4-2) to January 14, 2020.
(Councilmeber DeMarco & Passey opposed.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Faithful Life Church, applicant, by Robert D. Brant, Attorney-in-fact, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B for a Religious Institution (Faithful Life Church) in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 9870 Main St and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 58-3-02-013A, 013B, 013C.

 
Approved, unanimously to move that the City Council approve the request from Faithful Life Church, applicant, by Robert D. Brant, attorney-in-fact, for a special use permit to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B for a religious institution (Faithful Life Church) in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning district and Architectural Control Overlay district on the premises known as 9870 Main St and more particularly described as tax map parcel 58-3-02-013A, 013B, 013C, subject to the following conditions:
  1. The operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and plans. The Zoning administrator shall be enabled to approve minor changes to the time schedule.
  2. Noise levels shall comply with operational performance standards for noise in the Zoning Ordinance for commercial uses.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on amendments to the FY 2021 Budget Guidelines.

 
Approved, unanimously to move to adopt the proposed Budget Guidelines to be used in the preparation of the Fiscal Year 2021 Budget.

Consideration of a Purchase and Sale Agreement for the acquisition of certain real property owned by the Board of Supervisors of Fairfax County, Virginia, located within the City at 10635 and 10637 West Drive, comprising approximately 4.24 acres with improvements thereon, and having Tax Map No. 57-3-02-013 and 57-3-02-012.

 
Approved, unanimously to move to approve a Purchase and Sale Agreement between the City of Fairfax and the Board of Supervisors of Fairfax County for certain real property, with improvements, owned by the County Board and located at 10635 and 10637 West Drive within the City, substantially of the form attached to the Staff Report for this item.

Further move to authorize the Mayor and/or the City Manager to execute the Agreement on behalf of the City, provided that the final agreement is substantially of the form attached to the Staff Report for this item, subject to such non-substantive modifications that are approved by the City Attorney.
