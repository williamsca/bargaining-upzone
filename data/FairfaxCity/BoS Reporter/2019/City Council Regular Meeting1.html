a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Nuria Ibrahim Aman, applicant, for consideration of a renewal of Special Use Permit (SU-15020129) pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow a Family Home Daycare with up to 12 children maximum in the RH, Residential High Zoning District on the premises known as 10625 West Drive and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-3-02-018.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request from Nuria Ibrahim Aman, applicant, for consideration of a renewal of a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow a family home daycare with up to 12 children maximum in the RH, Residential High Zoning District on the premises known as 10625 West Drive and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-3-02-018, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The applicant shall care for a maximum of up to twelve children;
  2. The hours of operation shall be 7:00 am to 6:30 pm, Monday through Friday;
  3. No more than two employees, exclusive of the applicant, shall be permitted during the hours of childcare use;
  4. The garage shall be maintained for parking of two automobiles for the residential use;
  5. There shall be no drop-off or parking permitted on the 20-foot outlot road/alley.
  6. No signage pertaining to the child care business shall be permitted on the premises;
  7. The outdoor play area shall not be used for playing prior to 8:00 AM;
  8. There shall be no modifications to the exterior of the building which detract from the residential character of the site;
  9. The Special Use Permit shall be valid only for the applicant and shall not be transferrable to any other person; and
  10. The applicant shall maintain all licenses required by the state to operate.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from James Muccio, applicant, for consideration of a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow the keeping of chickens at 3723 Anne Place in RH (Residential High) Zoning District on the premises known as 3723 Anne Place and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-2-11-045.

 

Approved, 4-2, the request from James Muccio, applicant, for consideration of a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5.d.14 to allow the keeping of chickens at 3723 Anne Place in RH Residential High Zoning District on the premises known as 3723 Anne Place and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-2-11-045, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The keeping of chickens shall be consistent with application, statement of support and plans received October 30, 2018 and November 13, 2018;
  2. All pens must be kept dry, well-ventilated, and in a sanitary condition at all times, and must be cleaned on a regular basis to prevent offensive odors. All manure not used for composting or fertilizing shall be removed promptly. Odors from chickens, manure, or other chicken-related substances shall not be detectable at the property boundaries;
  3. The applicant shall ensure all activities associated with the care and treatment of hens shall be consistent with all City and Virginia laws, ordinances, and regulations;
  4. The applicant shall provide a written statement granting the approval of city staff to enter the property to inspect the property as it relates to the conditions of this special use permit;
  5. The special use permit shall be non-transferable; and
  6. Hens shall be attended by a person at all times while outside of the enclosure.

(Councilmembers Lim and Miller opposed.)
