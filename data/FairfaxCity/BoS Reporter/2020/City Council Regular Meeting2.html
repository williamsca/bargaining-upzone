 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request by Montessori School of Oakton, applicant, by Carolyn Linke, agent, for Special Use Permits pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5.C to allow a school and day care (as an accessory use) to a religious institution, on property in the RH, Residential High, CR, Commercial Retail zoning and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 10830 Main Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-15-B-005.

 
Approved, unanimously to move that the City Council approve the request by Montessori School of Oakton, applicant, by Carolyn Linke, agent, for Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5.C to allow a school and day care (as an accessory use) to a religious institution, on property in the RH, Residential High, CR, Commercial Retail Zoning and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 10830 Main Street, and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-15-B-005, subject to the following conditions:
  1. The applicant shall submit a shared parking agreement with Fairfax Baptist Church to the Zoning Administrator for approval prior to obtaining a use & occupancy permit.
  2. The Operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and plans. The Zoning Administrator shall be enabled to approve minor changes.
  3. The maximum combined number of students enrolled at the daycare and school use shall not exceed 125.
  4. Access from Walnut Street and First Street will both remain open at all times.
  5. The applicant shall provide and maintain where necessary, way finding signage to preschool and elementary school parking area and entrance, subject to approval by zoning.
  6. A drop off arrangement shall not be allowed; cars must be parked and students walked into school.
  7. All required parking for school and daycare uses shall occur onsite.
  8. Applicant shall obtain and maintain all local and State permits.
  9. The Special Use Permit shall be valid for three years and expire on February 25, 2023.

(Councilmember Lim absent)
