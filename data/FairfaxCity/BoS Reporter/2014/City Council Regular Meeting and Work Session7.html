 8.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of an award of contract to FieldTurfUSA, Inc. in an amount not to exceed $659,777.04 for the replacement of Fairfax High School synthetic turf.

 

 Approved, unanimously, award a contract to FieldTurfUSA, Inc. and authorize the City Manager to execute the same, in an amount not to exceed $659,777.04 for the replacement of Fairfax High School synthetic turf, provided that the contract and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney.
 
 

b.

Consideration to award a contract to F.H. Paschen Tectonics Corporation in an estimated amount of $4,049,638 for the Old Town Square project.

 
Failed by a voice vote of 4-3, to award a contract to F.H. Paschen Tectonics Corporation and authorize the City Manager to execute the same, in an estimated amount of $4,049,638 for the Old Town Square Project, provided that the contract and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney ( In favor, Councilmembers Drummond, Greenfield, and Stombres; opposed Mayor Silverthorne and Councilmembers DeMarco, Meyer, and Schmidt.) *This item was reconsidered following the closed meeting.*
 
 

c.

Consideration of a resolution to authorize City Manager to submit a letter of endorsement for the City’s 2015 application for the Transportation/Land-Use Connections (TLC) Program

 

Approved, unanimously, a resolution to authorize City Manager to submit a letter of endorsement for the City’s 2015 application for the Transportation/Land-Use Connections (TLC) Program.
