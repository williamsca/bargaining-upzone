7.

Public hearings.

 

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of the Virginia DeRoma, Owner, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-512 (12), to allow a major home occupation for a clinical psychology practice in the R-3 Residential District, on the premises known as 10933 Park Road and more particularly described as Tax Map parcel 57-1-13-007 A.

 
Approved, unanimously, the request of Virginia DeRoma, owner, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Sections 110-366 and 110-512(12), for a major home occupation (Psychology Practice) in the R-3 Residential District on the property located at 10933 Park Road, and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-13-007A, subject to the following conditions:
  1. The applicant shall limit the normal hours of operation to 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. daily.
  2. The applicant shall ensure that no more than one client/patient will be onsite at any time to allow for each client to remove his or her vehicle before the next client arrives.
  3. The applicant shall ensure that any client/patient vehicles will be parked onsite in the driveway of the residence.
(Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Navy Federal Credit Union, applicant, by Richard Pepper, Agent, for a special exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-185 to modify the requirements for building mounted signage for a shopping center located in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and Highway Corridor Overlay District on the premises known as 11180-82 Lee Highway (Jermantown Square Shopping Center), and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-02-016.

 
Approved, unanimously, to adopt a resolution to approve the request by Navy Federal Credit Union, Applicant, and Richard W. Pepper, Agent, for a special exception to section 110-180(b)(2)(a)2 to allow a tenant sign on a non-building-frontage façade of a shopping center building on the premises know as 11180-82 Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map 57-1-02-016, subject to the following conditions:
  1. The proposed signage and lighting shall be in general conformance with the exhibits included with the application by Navy Federal Credit Union, as revised through April 15, 2014.
  2. The total area of all building-mounted signage for this tenant space shall not exceed 80 square feet.
  3. Only signage for the tenant in 11180-82 Lee Highway shall be affixed to the south-facing façade wall. 
(Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

8.

Items not requiring a public hearing.

 

a.

Consideration of a request of Landmark Atlantic Capitol, LLC, by John H. Thillmann, Agent, for a subdivision ordinance variance to allow provision of curb and gutter along Old Lee Highway at the existing edge of pavement rather than increasing the paved width of the road as required by Section 86-7(1) and 86-8 on the land known as 3675 Old Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-2-02-188.

 
Approved, unanimously, the application of Landmark Atlantic Capitol, LLC, by John H. Thillmann, Agent, for a variance to the requirements of subdivision Code Sections 86-7 and 86-8 to permit a subdivision of land without providing the required pavement widening for the Old Lee Highway portion of the subject property known as 3675 Old Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-2-02-188 and find that:
  1. There are extraordinary circumstances wherein the application requirements of Chapter 86 of the City Code would result in peculiar and exceptional practical difficulty.
  2. The requested variance would be in conformant with the Comprehensive Plan.
  3. The requested variance can be granted without substantially impairing the intent and purpose of Chapter 86 of the City Code. 
(Councilman Greenfield recused himself from the dais andCouncilmember Schmidt was absent.)
