Fairfax High School.

 

Approved, unanimously, a request of Milestone Communications, Inc. and Verizon Wireless, applicants, by Frank W. Stearns, agent and counsel, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-482 (15) to:

1. Allow a telecommunications monopole up to 126-feet in height and ground equipment with screening at Fairfax High School in the R-2 Residential District and Highway Corridor Overlay District,

on the premises known as 3501 Rebel Run, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 58-1-02-003, subject to the following conditions:

  1. Development of the site shall be in substantial conformance with the attached Special Use Permit Plat dated June 11, 2014 and revised through November 13, 2014.
  2. The final location of the utility easement area shall not conflict with required landscaping areas or required trees.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of The Auld Shebeen, by Michael Boyle, applicant, for renewal of a Special Use Permit (SU-09090018) to allow dancing and entertainment in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and Old Town Fairfax Historic Overlay District pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(1) on the premises known as 3971 Chain Bridge Road, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-4-02-104.

 

Approved, unanimously, a request of The Auld Shebeen, by Michael Boyle, applicant, for renewal of a Special Use Permit (SU-09090018) pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(1) to:

1.   Allow dancing and entertainment in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and Old Town Fairfax Historic Overlay District,

on the premises known as 3971 Chain Bridge Road, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-4-02-104, subject to the following conditions:

  1. Dancing and live entertainment activities shall occur only during the hours of 10:00 p.m. to 1:30 a.m. Monday through Saturday and noon to 1:30 a.m. on Sunday, Federal holidays and Saint Patrickâ€™s Day.
  2. Dancing and entertainment activities shall be restricted to the designated 578 square foot dancing and entertainment area located on the lower level of the building.
  3. Operations of dancing and entertainment shall be in conformance with the statement of support and building plans as submitted on October 14, 2014.
