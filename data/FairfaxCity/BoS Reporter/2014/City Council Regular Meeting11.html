                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 24, 2014 
 
 1.  Invocation (Councilman DeMarco).
 
 2.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 3.  Presentations and Proclamations.  None.
 
 4.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 5.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 6.  Consent agenda.  None.
 
 7.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Section 98-1 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to state motor vehicle laws.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending and reenacting Section 98-1, Article 1, Chapter 98, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to incorporate by reference into the City Code certain state code provisions relating to the state motor vehicle laws, driving motor vehicles while intoxicated, and disposition of such cases for juveniles.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 110, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, as it pertaining to storm drainage facilities and erosion and sediment control.

 
Approved, unanimously, to enact an ordinance amending Chapter 110, Article II, Division 3, Division 11, Subdivisions I and II, and Division 12, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to storm water and storm drainage facilities, Chesapeake Bay preservation and erosion and sediment control.
 
 
 
 8.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

Consideration of a resolution approving the City of Fairfax Strategic Master Plan for parks, recreation, trails, open space, events, and cultural arts.

 
Approved, unanimously, to adopt the resolution approving the City of Fairfax Strategic Master Plan for parks, recreation, trails, open space, events, and cultural arts.
