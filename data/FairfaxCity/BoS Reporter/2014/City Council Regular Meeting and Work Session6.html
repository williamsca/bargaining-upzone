                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         April 8, 2014 
 
 1.  Invocation.  (Councilman DeMarco)
 
 2.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 3.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation by the Chocolate Lovers Committee of the Chocolate Lovers Festival Annual Grant.

 
 
 4.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 5.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 6.  Consent agenda.

a.

Introduction of an ordinance amending Chapter 90, Article II, Division 3 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to real property tax exemptions, deferrals and freezes for certain elderly or disabled taxpayers. (Public hearing April 22, 2014.)

 

b.

Introduction of an ordinance amending Chapter 70, Article II, Section 70-36 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to the calculation of rental relief for elderly or disabled persons. (Public hearing April 22, 2014.)

 

c.

Introduction of an Ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute an agreement with Layton Hall Limited Partnership, to install and maintain two (2) storm sewer outfalls within a portion of the City’s right-of-way for a period of five (5) years. (Public hearing April 22, 2014.)

 

d.

Introduction of an ordinance to affect the financing required for the development and improvement of City owned downtown properties, to include the City’s Old Town Square Park, the East Street parking lot and the North Street pedestrian barrier, and the renovation and expansion of the City’s Police Department Firearms Training Center located on Pickett Road. (Public hearing April 22, 2014.)

 

e.

Introduction of an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 90, Article II, Division 1, Section 90-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax used for or zoned for commercial and industrial purposes, and to establish a separate tax rate on such real property effective as of January 1, 2013, revenues from which would be used exclusively for transportation purposes that benefit the City of Fairfax. (Public hearing April 30, 2014.)

 

f.

Introduction of an ordinance amending Section 102-31 of the code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to increase the Wastewater Utility rate by 10% for Fiscal Year 2014-2015.  (Public hearing April 30, 2014.)

 

g.

Introduction of an appropriation resolution in the amount not to exceed $8,100,000 for the development and improvement of City owned downtown properties, to include the City’s Old Town Square Park, the East Street parking lot and the North Street pedestrian barrier, and the renovation and expansion of the City’s Police Department Firearms Training Center located on Pickett Road.  (Public hearing April 22, 2014)

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda.  (Councilman DeMarco was absent.)
 
 
 
 7.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing on the City Manager's proposed FY 2014-2015 budget.
No Council action required.

 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Premier Suites, LLC by Inda Stagg, agent, for a Proffered Condition Amendment to allow specific non-office uses to be located on any of the six floors, limited to the same gross square footage as approved previously on the land known as 10560 Main Street and more particularly described as tax map 57-2-42-001.

 
Approved unanimously, an ordinance to approve the application of Premier Suites LLC by Inda Stagg, agent, for a Proffered Condition Amendment on the premises located at 10560 Main Street and more particularly described as tax map 57-2-42-001. (Councilman DeMarco was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriate resolution in the amount of $60,000 to complete Phase One of the Police Department Firearms Training Center project and direct staff to proceed with the project.

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $60,000 to begin phase one of the police firearms training center project. (Councilman DeMarco was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Items not requiring a public hearing.  None.

March 25, 2014 regular meeting.
