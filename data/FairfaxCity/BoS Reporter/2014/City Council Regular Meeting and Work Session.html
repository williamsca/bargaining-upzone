 7.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Fairfax Plaza LLC by Mehdi Sarbaz, manager, to rezone a 25,916 square-foot portion of a site from C-2, Retail Commercial and I-2, Industrial to C-2(p), Retail Commercial (with proffers) and for Special Exception to reduce the width of the front landscape strip adjacent to the public right-of-way (Sec.110-259(a)(1)a), and  for a Special Exception to allow individual businesses to be identified on a shopping center identification ground-mounted sign (Sec.110-180 (b) (2) a.1) on the land known as 9538 Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map 48-3-01-031A. (next to Arthur Treachers).

 
Approved, 5-1, to adopt an ordinance to approve the application of  Fairfax Plaza,  LLC, by Mehdi Sarbaz, property  owner, to rezone the subject property from C-2 Retail Commercial and I-2, Industrial to C-2(p), Retail Commercial with proffers dated  December 30, 2013 on the premises known as 9538 Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map 48-3-01-031a.  (In favor: Councilmembers DeMarco, Drummond, Greenfield, Schmidt and Stombres; opposed, Councilman Meyer.

 

Approved, 5-1, to adopt a resolution to approve the request by Fairfax Plaza, LLC, for special exceptions to Section 110-259(a)(1)a, to reduce the width of the front landscape strip adjacent to the public right-of-way and Section 110-180 (b) (2) a.1 to modify the restriction to allow individual businesses to be identified on a shopping center identification ground-mounted sign on the premises known as 9538 Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map 48-3-01-031A, subject to the following condition:

1.       Development shall be in substantial conformance with the proffered Generalized Development Plan revised through October 9, 2013.

In favor: Councilmembers DeMarco, Drummond, Greenfield, Schmidt and Stombres; opposed, Councilman Meyer.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance vacating a portion of a City waterline easement in Loudoun County, Virginia, located on lands of Section 69-Ashburn Village.

 
Approved, unanimously, to defer action on an ordinance authorizing the vacation of a portion of a City waterline easement in Loudoun County, Virginia, to January 28, 2014.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $61,755 to fund the Ashby Pond Conservatory Trail project.

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $61,755 to fund the Ashby Pond Conservatory Trail project for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2013.
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 54, Article V, Division 1, Section 54-175 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to unlawful hunting.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 54, Article V, Division 1, Section 54-175 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to unlawful hunting.
