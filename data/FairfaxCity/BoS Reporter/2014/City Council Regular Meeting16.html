 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a requests of Pickett LLC, owner and applicant, by Molly Novotny, agent, for a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-185 to allow building mounted signage in a shopping center (Pickett Shopping Center) to exceed the maximum allowable sign area and be located on non-building frontage walls; and to allow a ground-mounted shopping center identification sign to exceed the maximum height and square footage and identify the names of individual tenants, in the C-2 Retail Commercial District on the premises known as 9400-9490 Main Street as more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 58-3-02-029.

 
Approved, unanimously,  a requests of Pickett LLC, owner and applicant, by Molly Novotny, agent, for Special Exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-185 to:
  1. Modify the requirements of City Code Sections 110-180 (b)(2) (a) (2)(i) and (ii) to allow building mounted shopping center tenant signage on retail and non-retail units that exceeds the 100 and 150 square-foot maximum limit; to allow building mounted shopping center tenant signage on retail and non-retail units that exceeds 1.5 square feet per linear foot of building frontage for retail tenants and 1.0 square feet per linear foot of building frontage for non-retail tenants; and to allow building mounted signage on walls not defined as building frontage; and
  2. Modify the requirements of City Code Sections 110-180(b)(2)(a)(1) and 110-181(b) to allow a ground mounted shopping center identification sign that exceeds the 50 square foot area limit and 6-foot height limit, and that identifies individual tenants,
on the premises known as 9400-9490 Main Street as more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 58-3-02-029 subject to the following conditions:
  1. All signage shall be in substantial conformance with the attached Special Exception Master Sign Plan prepared by JP2 Architects, LLC, dated September 8, 2014 and the Sign Plan Matrix dated July 7, 2014 and revised through September 11, 2014.
  2. The building-mounted signage for the two anchor tenants shall not exceed 200 square feet.
  3. Building-mounted signage for the two end caps (currently Total Wine and AAA) shall not exceed 1.65 square feet per linear foot up to a maximum of 165 square feet on each wall;
  4. No more than one under canopy blade sign shall be permitted for each business, and each blade sign shall not exceed four square feet.
  5. The ground mounted identification sign shall not exceed 130 square feet of area per side and 20 feet in height.
  6. Prior to issuance of the sign permits, the applicant shall remove the main shopping center pylon sign and the applicant shall remove the Einstein Bros Bagel pylon sign prior to receiving an occupancy permit for a new tenant in that space.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $14,841 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Downtown Fund to fund the Old Town Traffic Study.
