City of Fairfax, Virginia 22030-3630

                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 27, 2023 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.

a.

Consideration for remote participation by Councilmember Stehle pursuant to Code of Virginia Sec. 2.2-3708.2.

 
Approved, unanimously for remote participation by Councilmember Stehle
 
 
 
 2.  Moment of Silence
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance to the Flag.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation acknowledging the month of July as Park and Recreation Month.

 
 
 5.  General public comment (a maximum of three (3) minutes for any items not requiring a public hearing).
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
Approved, unanimously to amend agenda with switching agenda items #11b to #11c and #11c to #11b.
 
 
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of the June 13, 2023 Regular Meeting minutes.

 

b.

Consideration of board and commission reappointments.

 

c.

Consideration of approval on amendments to the Fairfax City Purchasing Resolution.

 

d.

Consideration to authorize the City Manager to execute a Sewer Billing Agreement with Fairfax Water Authority.

 
Approved, unanimously the consent agenda.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing the City, for indefinite delivery or quantity contracts meeting the requirements of Code of Virginia §2.2-4337(A), to allow the contractor awarded such contract to furnish to the City a performance bond and a payment bond, each of which shall be equal to the dollar amount of the individual tasks identified in the underlying contract.

 
Approved, unanimously to enact an ordinance authorizing the City, for indefinite delivery or quantity contracts meeting the requirements of enabling authority in the Code of Virginia, to require and except performance and payment bonds, each of which shall be equal to the dollar amount of the individual tasks identified in such contracts.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $30,000 for the City Jobs program for FY 2023.

 
Approved, 4-2, Councilmembers Doyle Feingold and Greenfield opposed, to approve an appropriation resolution in the amount of $30,000 for FY 2023 for the City Jobs Program.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease for a term commencing July 1, 2023 and ending June 30, 2026 by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia, and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc., for approximately 7,364 square feet of space in the former Green Acres Elementary School site, located at 4401 Sideburn Road.

 
Approved, unanimously to enact an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease between the City of Fairfax and Main Street Childcare, Inc., for property located at 4401 Sideburn Road.
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a License Agreement between Carolinas Child Care, LLC and the City of Fairfax, Virginia for a portion of the City’s Burrows Avenue right of way located adjacent to 3405 Burrows Avenue.

 
Approved, unanimously to enact an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a License Agreement between the City of Fairfax and Carolinas Child Care, LLC, for a portion of the City’s Burrows Avenue right-of-way adjacent to 3405 Burrows Avenue.
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.  None.
 
 10.  Recess the regular meeting to go into a work session.

a.

Discussion on a request of Capital City Real Estate for redevelopment of 10306 Eaton Place.

 

b.

Discussion and introduction on the Fairfax Circle Small Area Plan.

 

c.

Discussion on the proposed Northern Virginia Transportation Authority (NVTA) 70% Funding Application for Northfax East-West Roadway.

 
 
 12.  Reconvene the regular meeting.
 
 13.  City Manager report out (if needed).
 
 14.  Council Comments and Committee reports out (if needed).
 
 15.  Closed meeting (if needed).
 
 16.  Adjournment.
