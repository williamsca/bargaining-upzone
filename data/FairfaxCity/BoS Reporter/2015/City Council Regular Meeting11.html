 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on proposed amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 to modify requirements of the RPD Residential Planned Development District pertaining to permitted uses, special exceptions and procedures.

 
Approved, 5-1, that the City Council approve the proposed text amendments to City Code Chapter 110, Article X, Section 110-671, Section 110-672, Section 110-673, Section 110-674, and deletion of City Code Chapter 110, Article X, Section 110-680 as provided in the attached ordinance dated April 8, 2015.  (In favor: Councilmembers DeMarco, Greenfield, Loftus, Miller, and Schmidt; opposed, Councilman Meyer.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a Request of Oak Knolls Realty Corporation, by Lynne J. Strobel, Attorney-in Fact, for a rezoning of 6.10 acres from RM Multifamily to RPD Residential Planned Development with proffers; for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-366 to allow multifamily uses in a Residential Planned Development district, for  Special Exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-673 to allow for a reduction of the 25 foot minimum open space around the perimeter of the subject property and a reduction of the 20 foot minimum front and rear yards of individual lots, to allow structures to exceed the 35 foot height limitation, and to allow impervious area to exceed 50% of the gross tract area, for a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-158 to allow modifications to residential parking area requirements, for a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-105(k) to allow utilities to be relocated in an adjacent right-of-way without being undergrounded, and for Variances to the Subdivision Ordinance pursuant to Section 86-10 to allow for a private street without sidewalks on both sides and private accessways to be less than 24 feet in width, on the land known as 10758 to 10782 Main Street, tax map parcel  57-1-02-116.

 
Approved, unanimously, that the City Council adopt an ordinance to approve the application of Oak Knolls Realty Corporation, by Lynne J. Strobel, agent, to rezone the subject property RM-Residential Multifamily to RPD-Residential Planned Development with proffers; subject to the general development plan dated March 27, 2014 and last revised on May 11, 2015 and proffers dated May 26, 2015, and further revised at 9:55 p.m. on May 26, 2015, on the land known as 10742 to 10764 Main Street, more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-02-116. Approved, unanimously, the request of Oak Knolls Realty Corporation LLC, by Lynne J. Strobel, agent, for variances to the Subdivision Ordinance pursuant to Section 86-10 to allow private accessways to be less than thirty (30) feet in width, and to allow private accessways without sidewalks on both sides, on the premises knows as 10742 to 10764 Main Street, more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-02-116 subject to the following condition:
  1. Development of the subject site shall be in substantial conformance with the attached GDP/Preliminary Site Plan last revised May 11, 2015 and as may be modified by the City Council.
Approved, unanimously, to adopt a resolution to approve with conditions the special exception requests of Oak Knolls Realty Corporation, by Lynne J. Strobel, agent, to:
  1. Reduce the required perimeter open space width and minimum front and rear yard setbacks for individual residential units;
  2. Increase the maximum building height to more than three stories or 35 feet;
  3. Allow impervious area to exceed 50% of gross tract area;
  4. Modify access requirements for individual parking spaces; and,
  5. Allow utilities to be relocated in an adjacent right-of-way without being undergrounded.
for redevelopment of the property being rezoned to RPD-Residential Planned Development with proffers, located at 10742 to 10764 Main Street, more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-02-116 with the following condition:
1.         Development of the subject site shall be in substantial conformance with the attached GDP/Preliminary Site Plan last revised May 11, 2015 and as may be modified by the City Council.
