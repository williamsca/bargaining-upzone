 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Rise and Shine Daycare, applicant, by Monica Downs, President, for the renewal of a Special Use Permit, pursuant to City Code Section 110-512 (17), to allow a child care center as an accessory to a church (Fairfax Christian Church) in the R-3 Residential District on the premises known as 10185 Main Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-4-09-001.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Rise and Shine Child Care, applicant, by Monica Downs, President, for renewal of a special use permit, pursuant to City Code Section 110-512 (17), to allow a child care center as an accessory to a church in the R-3 Residential District on the premises known as 10185 Main Street, and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-4-09-001 subject to the following conditions:

  1. The applicant shall provide child care services for a maximum of 35 children;
  2. No more than six employees, exclusive of the applicant, shall be permitted;
  3. During periods of drop-offs and pick-ups, vehicles shall be parked in parking spaces and children escorted into and out of the building;
  4. The outdoor play area shall not be used for playing prior to 8:00 AM; the four-foot high fence enclosure around a 1,400 square outdoor play area and shown on the plan dated September 16, 2012 shall remain;
  5. The applicant shall work closely with the church to ensure that additional activities at the church during the hours of operation of the child care will not require more parking than provided.
  6. All required permits and licenses for the child care center operation shall be maintained at all times; and
  7. The Special Use Permit shall be valid only for the applicant, shall not be transferrable to any other person and shall expire on October 13, 2020.
(Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Team Washington, Inc., by Susan Fulton, Agent, for Special Use Permits pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(5) to allow the operation of specialty food establishment that provides delivery service; and pursuant to City Code Section 110-874 (b) (3) to allow a commercial use seeking vehicular access from a local street which also provides access to residential areas in the  C-2 Retail Commercial and HCOD Highway Corridor Overlay Districts on the premises known as 10895 Main Street, and more particularly described  as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-22-005. (Domino’s Pizza)

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Team Washington Inc., by Susan Fulton, agent, for special use permits pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(5), to allow a specialty food establishment that provides delivery service; and pursuant to City Code Section 110-874(3), to allow a commercial use seeking vehicular access from a local street which also provides access to residential areas in the C-2 Retail Commercial and HCOD Highway Corridor Overlay Districts on the premises known as 10895 Main Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-22-005 subject to the following conditions:

  1. Delivery drivers shall use Main Street, at its intersection with Hallman and Maple Street, as the primary entrance and exit routes for delivery trips outside of the Westmore neighborhood;     
  2. The applicant shall limit the number of delivery employees to a maximum of four during any shift prior to 6:00 p.m.;
  3. The hours of operation shall be limited to 10:00 a.m. to midnight, Sunday through Thursday; and 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m., Friday and Saturday;
  4. The Special Use Permit approval expires in five years.
(Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending regulations for City of Fairfax Parks, per the City Code, Chapter 58, Article III, Section 58-61, Park Regulations .

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance adopting revised rules and regulations pertaining to parks and other open space facilities owned and operated by the City of Fairfax, Virginia. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
