 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $5,206.23 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Downtown Fund to fund preliminary engineering and traffic analysis for modifications to University Drive.

 
Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation in the amount of $5,206.23 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Downtown Fund to fund the University Drive roadway design and traffic analysis project.(Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $302,326 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund to fund construction for the Fairfax Boulevard Kamp Washington to Fairchester Roadbed Reconstruction Project.

 
Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation in the amount of $302,326 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund to fund construction for the Fairfax Boulevard Roadbed Reconstruction Project from Kamp Washington to Fairchester Drive.(Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $995,000 for funding awarded to the City through VDOT’s FY 16 Primary Extension Paving Program.

 
Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation in in the amount of $995,000 for funding awarded to the City through VDOT’s FY16 Primary Extension Paving Program.(Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $30,000 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund to fund the George T. Snyder Trail project.

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation in the amount of $30,000 for the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund to fund the George T. Snyder Trail Project. (Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
 
 

e.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Section 98-1 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to state motor vehicle laws.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending and reenacting Section 98-1, Article 1, Chapter 98, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to incorporate by reference into the City Code certain state code provisions relating to the state motor vehicle laws, driving motor vehicles while intoxicated, and disposition of such cases for juveniles.
