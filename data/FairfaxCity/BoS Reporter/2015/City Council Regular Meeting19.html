a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Red Zone Grill LLC, applicant, by Tarik Hassan, Owner, for renewal of special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(1) to allow dancing and entertainment as uses ancillary to a restaurant (Red Zone Grill) in the C-2(p) Retail Commercial and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay Districts on the premises known as 10560 Main Street (Mosby Building) and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-2-((42))-001.

 

Approved, unanimously, a request of Red Zone Grill LLC, applicant, by Tarik Hassan, Owner, for renewal of special use permit to allow dancing and entertainment as uses ancillary to a restaurant (Red Zone Grill) in the C-2(p) Retail Commercial and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay Districts on the premises known as 10560 Main Street (Mosby Building) and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-2-((42))-001, subject to the following conditions:

  1. Red Zone Grill management shall maintain control of all activities on the premises at all times when the restaurant is open and shall ensure that the premises are never occupied by more than 299 persons, and shall ensure that the primary use of the premises at all times is a restaurant.
  2. Dancing and live entertainment shall be limited to the hours of 8:00 p.m. to 1:30 a.m., Tuesday through Saturday accessory to the restaurant use.
  3. Dancing and entertainment activities shall be restricted to two areas: the 22 feet x 23 feet (506 square feet) area and the 25 feet x 17 (425 square feet) depicted on the proposed floor plan.
  4. The applicant shall maintain a parking agreement with the building owner/management guaranteeing 123 parking spaces dedicated to Red Zone Grill from the hours of 8:00 pm to 2:00 am.
  5. The applicant shall restrict patrons to parking in the 157 spaces provided in the parking lot adjacent to Main Street.
  6. The applicant shall attend meetings upon the request of Main Street Green Condominium Association to discuss and resolve any issues that might arise from the restaurant’s operation and shall document the discussions and promptly submit such documentation to the City.
  7. Garbage in the parking lot area shall be removed immediately after close of business every night.
  8. Every six months following the date of approval of this Special Use Permit, for a period of 24 months, the applicant shall report to City staff on the events that have occurred in the restaurant and all known violations and complaints.
