                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         October 27, 2015 
 
 1.  Call the Regular Meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilman Meyer).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.  None.
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of an MOU between the City of Fairfax and the City of Fairfax Volunteer Fire Department.

 

Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Kwang and Lee Property, LLC, applicant, by Kenneth K. Lee, Manager, for a Special Exception, pursuant to City Code Section 110-845 (6), to allow a fence within the front building restriction area of a lot in the I-2 Industrial District on the premises known as 3159 Campbell Drive, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 48-3-09-025A.  (Northern Virginia Auto Body.)

 

Approved, unanimously, to adopt a resolution to approve the request of Kwang and Lee Property, LLC, by Kenneth K. Lee, manager, for a special exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-845(6), to allow a fence within the front building restriction area of a lot in the I-2 Industrial District on the premises known as 3159 Campbell Drive, and more particularly described as tax map parcel 48-3-09-025a subject to the following conditions:

  1. Construction of the fence shall be in substantial conformance with the attached Special Exception Plat (Exhibit D) received October 1, 2015.
  2. Prior to installation of the fence, the applicant shall replace all dead trees along the front property line and shall install evergreen shrubs and an evergreen tree along the rear property and dumpster area in accordance with the Revision –Plantings plan dated September 30, 2015.
  3. No use or activity shall be permitted in approved landscape areas (such as vehicle access path into the building on the Roanoke property and/or storage of materials/vehicles).
(Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 26 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to add a new section 26-6 pertaining to the assessment of a fee as part of the costs in each criminal and traffic case in the general district court of the City, to be utilized to fund the development and costs of an electronic summons system.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 26 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to add a new Section 26-6 imposing a new fee to be charged in criminal and traffic cases in the General District Court of the City, to be utilized solely to implement and maintain an electronic summons system. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in an amount not to exceed $1,250,000 to allow for the purchase and financing of a fire truck, body type - Tower Ladder.  The fire truck to be replaced has exceeded its useful life and is scheduled for replacement.

 
Approved unanimously, a supplemental appropriation in the amount not to exceed $1,250,000 to allow for the purchase and financing of a fire truck, body type – tower ladder. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a resolution that (a) approves the lease/purchase of a fire truck and related equipment through a financing with TD Equipment Finance, Inc., (b) approves the form of an equipment lease agreement with the financing lessor and (c) approves a sublease of the fire truck and related equipment to the Fairfax Volunteer Fire Department.

 
Approved, unanimously, a resolution that (a) approves the lease/purchase of a fire truck and related equipment through a financing with TD Equipment Finance, Inc., (b) approves the form of an equipment lease agreement with the financing lessor and (c) approves a sublease of the fire truck and related equipment to the Fairfax Volunteer Fire Department. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
