 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of IDI-RJL Development, LLC, by David Houston, Attorney-in Fact, for a General Development Plan Amendment with proffers for the 9.10 acre Mantua Professional Center Commercial Planned Development; for Special Exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-704 to allow for an increase in the maximum building height from 60 feet to 75 feet, and an increase in the maximum gross density for the General Development Plan area from 4 dwelling units per acre to 8.8 dwelling units per acre with a commercial ratio of less than 1,000 square feet per residential unit; a Special Exception pursuant to Section 110-263 to allow a landscape strip of less than 7 feet in width between an off-street parking area and a property line; for Variances to the Subdivision Ordinance pursuant to Section 86-10 to allow for a private street without sidewalks on both sides and private accessways to be less than 24 feet in width; and for a Comprehensive Plan Amendment to change the future land use designation from Business - Commercial to Mixed Use on the land known as 9431 to 9495 Silver King Court, inclusive, otherwise known as tax map parcels 58-2-10-01-001 through 58-2-10-01-007, 58-2-10-001-B, 58-2-10-001-C and 58-2-10-02-001-A.

 
Approved, unanimously, resolution amending of the Comprehensive Plan of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to classify as mixed-use on the future land use map the property identified as City of Fairfax tax map parcels 58-2-10-01-001 through 58-2-10-01-007, 58-2-10-001-B, 58-2-10-001-C and 58-2-10-02-001-A, comprising approximately 9.12 acres.   Approved, unanimously, an ordinance to approve the application of IDI-RJL Development, lLC, by David Houston, Attorney-in-Fact, for a Proffer Plan/General Development Plan Amendment; subject to the General Development Plan revised September 1, 2015 and further revised on September 8, 2015, and proffers revised September 1, 2015, on the land known as 9431 to 9495 Silver King Court, inclusive, and more particularly described as tax map parcels 58-2-10-01-001 through 58-2-10-01-007, 58-2-10-001-B, 58-2-10-001-C and 58-2-10-02-001-A, with the following conditions.
  1. Development of the subject site shall be in substantial conformance with the attached plans dated December 23, 2014 revised on September 1, 2015, and further revised on September 8, 2015, as may be modified by the City Council.
  2. At initial construction, the development shall include no more than sixteen (16) three-bedroom units and no fewer than (16) one-bedroom units.
  3. No further parking reductions shall be permitted in the subject CPD area so long as the reciprocal parking agreement as defined in note 23 on Sheet 1 of the GDP is in place.
  Approved, unanimously, the request of IDI-RJL Development, LLC, by David Houston, Attorney-in-Fact, for variances to the Subdivision Ordinance pursuant to Section 86-10 to allow private accessways to be less than thirty (30) feet in width, and to allow private accessways without sidewalks on both sides, on the premises knows as 9431 to 9495 Silver King Court, inclusive, and more particularly described as tax map parcels 58-2-10-01-001 through 58-2-10-01-007, 58-2-10-001-B, 58-2-10-001-C and 58-2-10-02-001-A subject to the following conditions:
  1. Development of the subject site shall be in substantial conformance with the attached plans dated December 23, 2014, revised on September 1, 2015, and further revised on September 8, 2015, as may be modified by the City Council.
  Approved, unanimously, a resolution to approve with conditions the special exception requests of IDI-RJL Development, LLC, by David Houston, Attorney-in-Fact, to:
  1. Increase the maximum building height to more than five stories or 60 feet;
  2. Increase the maximum density to more than four dwelling units per acre and allow less than 1,000 square feet of office space per residential unit for the entirety of the commercial planned development;
  3. Allow residential parking to be located on a separate parcel from the principal use per section 110-152 (c); and
  4. Allow parking adjacent to a property line without the required landscape buffer.
for development of the property located at 9431 to 9495 Silver King Court, inclusive, and more particularly described as tax map parcels 58-2-10-01-001 through 58-2-10-01-007, 58-2-10-001-B, 58-2-10-001-C and 58-2-10-02-001-A with the following conditions:
  1. Development of the subject site shall be in substantial conformance with the attached plans dated December 23, 2014 and last revised on September 1, 2015, as may be modified by the City Council.

c.

Public hearing and Council action on Planning Commission findings and recommendations regarding blighted conditions on property located at 4261 Berritt Street, otherwise known as tax map parcel 57-4-12-032 and consideration of the city's plan to abate the blight as follows:
Remove aboveground pool equipment, drain pool and remove pool liner, remove fencing to allow equipment access to pool area, demolish concrete pool surround and coping to below grade, fill entire pool area to create a level area, place seed and straw over bare soil.

 

Approved, unanimously, the Planning Commission findings and recommendations regarding blighted conditions on property located at 4261 Berritt Street, otherwise known as tax map parcel 57-4-12-032 and consideration of the city’s plan to abate the blight as follows:

Remove aboveground pool equipment, drain pool and remove pool liner, remove fencing to allow equipment access to pool area, demolish concrete pool surround and coping to below grade, fill entire pool area to create a level area, place seed and straw over bare soil.
