                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         January 27, 2015 
 
 1.  Call the Regular Meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilman DeMarco).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation honoring the Kiwanis Club on their 100th Anniversary.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Initiation of a zoning text amendment amending Chapter 110 Article II, Division 11, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to storm drainage facilities.

 

b.

Initiation of a zoning text amendment amending Chapter 110 Article II, Division 2, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to the floodplain.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda.  (Mayor Silverthorne was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on Requests of JDC Fairfax Garden LLC, owner and applicant, by Francis X. Watkins, agent, for  Special Exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-185 to allow a ground-mounted shopping center identification sign to identify the names of individual tenants and be located closer than 10 feet from the front property line and to allow a United States flag to exceed the maximum allowable flag size of 24 square feet, in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and Highway Corridor Overlay District (HCOD) on the premises known as 9940 Fairfax Boulevard (Fairfax Boulevard Crossing) or as more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 48-3-14-001-A. (Formerly Cho’s Garden).

 
Approved, unanimously, the requests of JDC Fairfax Garden LLC, owner and applicant, by Francis X. Watkins, agent, for special exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-185 to allow a ground-mounted shopping center identification sign to identify the names of individual tenants and be located closer than 10 feet from the front property line and to allow a United States flag to exceed the maximum allowable flag size of 24 square feet, in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and Highway Corridor Overlay District (HCOD) on the premises known as 9940 Fairfax Boulevard (Fairfax Boulevard Crossing) or as more particularly described as tax map parcel 48-3-14-001-A subject to the following conditions:
1. The proposed monument sign shall be in substantial conformance with the attached Special Exception Plat (prepared by Walter L. Phillips and dated 01-06-2015) and Flag Plan (prepared by Bignell Watkins Hasser and dated 9-25-2012) and Double-Sided Monument Sign Plan sheet A-1.1 (prepared by Bignell Watkins Hasser and dated 10-30-2014).
2. The ground mounted monument sign not exceed six feet in height, six feet two inches in overall length, and 12 square feet in tenant sign area nor be located closer than 5.6 feet from the site’s front property line.
3.  The American flag approved to fly from the site’s flag pole shall not exceed 40 square feet in area (5’ x 8’) and shall preclude the use of any other flags.
(Mayor Silverthorne was absent.)
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of a resolution authorizing the City Manager to enter into an agreement with Fairfax County Water Authority to provide wastewater billing services for the City’s Wastewater Utility Fund.

 
Approved, unanimously, a resolution authorizing the City Manager to execute the following sewer billing agreement between the City of Fairfax, Virginia and the Fairfax County Water Authority, provided that the agreement and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney.  (Mayor Silverthorne was absent.)
