City of Fairfax, Virginia 22030-3630

                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         April 25, 2023 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Moment of Silence
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance to the Flag.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation acknowledging May as Bike Month and May 19, 2023 as Bike to Work Day in the City of Fairfax.

 
 
 5.  General public comment (a maximum of three (3) minutes for any items not requiring a public hearing).
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
Approved, unanimously the adoption of the agenda.
 
 
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of the April 11, 2023 Regular Meeting minutes.

 

b.

Consideration of a resolution renaming of Ranger Road Park to Cardinal Park.

 

c.

Consideration of amendment to the City of Fairfax Homelessness Task Force membership.

 
Approved, unanimously the consent agenda.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing on the Real Estate Tax of $1.040 per $100 of assessed value based upon an assessment ratio of 100% of fair market value, and a personal property and machinery and tools tax rate of $4.13 per $100 of assessed value based upon an assessment ratio of 100% trade-in value. (No Council action, tax rate set on May 2, 2023.)

 

b.

Public hearing on an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 90, Article II, Division I, Section 90-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax used for or zoned for commercial and industrial purposes, and to establish a separate tax rate on such real property effective as of January 1, 2023, revenues from which would be used exclusively for transportation purposes that benefit the City of Fairfax. (No Council action.)

 

c.

Public hearing on amending Chapter 90 (Taxation), Article X (Old Town Service District Tax) of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to the additional tax imposed upon real property and improvements located within the Old Town Service District. (No Council action.)

 

d.

Public hearing on an ordinance amending Chapter 102, Article V, Section 102-203 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to set the rate for stormwater utility service furnished by the City at $30.52 per Billing Unit (BU). (No Council action.)

 

e.

Public hearing on an ordinance amending Chapter 102, Article II, Section 102-31 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to adjust the rates for wastewater service furnished by the City by 6.0 percent. (No Council action.)

 

f.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $2,462,540 for the 3rd Quarter FY 2023 recommended expenditures.

 
Approved, 4-2, Councilmembers Doyle Feingold and Greenfield opposed, to approve an appropriation resolution in the amount of $2,462,540 to fund the FY 2023 Third Quarter recommended supplemental expenditures.
 
 

g.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing “quick-take” condemnation of certain public right-of-way in fee simple, together with various permanent and temporary easements, on real property owned by Fairfax Court Limited Partnership at 4040 Jermantown Road, and having Tax Map No. 56-2-02-002.

 
Approved, unanimously to enact an ordinance authorizing the initiation of "quick-take" condemnation proceedings for the acquisition of certain public right-of-way in fee simple, together with various permanent and temporary easements, on real property owned by Fairfax Court Limited Partnership, located at 4040 Jermantown Road, and having tax map no. 56-2-02-002.
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.  None
 
 10.  Recess the regular meeting to go into a work session.

a.

Discussion on the Fire Station #3 Feasibility Study.

 

b.

Discussion on the proposed FY24 budget.

 
 
 12.  Reconvene the regular meeting.
 
 13.  City Manager report out (if needed).
 
 14.  Council Comments and Committee reports out (if needed).
 
 15.  Closed meeting (if needed).
 
 16.  Adjournment.
