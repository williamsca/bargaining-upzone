                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 23, 2015 
 
 1.  Call the Regular Meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember Schmidt).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of Certificates of Appreciation for members of the Committee for Commemoration of the Sesquicentennial of the American Civil War.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.

a.

Update from Saul Holdings regarding Shoppes of Fairfax.

 
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.  None.
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on proposed amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 to modify requirements of the CPD Commercial Planned Development District pertaining to permitted uses, special exceptions, parking reductions and procedures.

 
Approved, unanimously, text amendments to City Code Chapter 110, Article XI, Section 110-701, Section 110-704, Section 110-705, Section 110-706, and deletion of City Code Chapter 110, Article XI, Section 110-711 as provided in the attached ordinance dated June 8, 2015.(Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of IDI-RJL Development, LLC, by David Houston, Attorney-in Fact, for a General Development Plan Amendment with proffers for the 9.10 acre Mantua Professional Center Commercial Planned Development; for Special Exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-704 to allow for an increase in the maximum building height from 60 feet to 75 feet, and an increase in the maximum gross density for the General Development Plan area from 4 dwelling units per acre to 8.8 dwelling units per acre with a commercial ratio of less than 1,000 square feet per residential unit; a Special Exception pursuant to Section 110-263 to allow a landscape strip of less than 7 feet in width between an off-street parking area and a property line; for Variances to the Subdivision Ordinance pursuant to Section 86-10 to allow for a private street without sidewalks on both sides and private accessways to be less than 24 feet in width; and for a Comprehensive Plan Amendment to change the future land use designation from Business - Commercial to Mixed Use on the land known as 9431 to 9495 Silver King Court, inclusive, otherwise known as tax map parcels 58-2-10-01-001 through 58-2-10-01-007, 58-2-10-001-B, 58-2-10-001-C and 58-2-10-02-001-A.

 
Approved, unanimously, to defer the public hearing and Council action on the request of IDI-RJL Development, LLC, by David Houston, Attorney-in Fact, for a General Development Plan Amendment with proffers to September 8, 2015. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a proposed sidewalk project on University Drive involving construction of approximately 150 linear feet of sidewalk on the west side of University Drive between Armstrong Street and South Street.

 
Approved, unanimously, to direct staff to consider changes to the proposed project consistent with the comments received from the City Council and the public, finalize the plans and proceed to right of way acquisition and construction.(Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a proposed sidewalk and storm drainage improvement project on Burke Station Road involving construction of approximately 1,100 linear feet of sidewalk, curb and gutter on the west side of Burke Station Road between Main Street and Stoughton Road.

 
Approved, unanimously, to consider changes to the proposed project consistent with the comments received from the City Council and the public, finalize the plans and proceed to right of way acquisition and construction. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

e.

Public hearing and Council action on a proposed traffic signal upgrade on Fairfax Boulevard at Draper Drive. This project will upgrade the traffic signal by installing new signal heads, wiring, span cable, controller cabinets, detector loops, signs, and service disconnect box.

 
Approved, unanimously, to direct staff to consider changes to the proposed project consistent with the comments received from the City Council and the public, finalize the plans and proceed to right of way acquisition and construction. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

f.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $96,335 for FY 2014-2015 Federal and State Grant Expenditures.

 
Approved, unanimously, to approve an appropriation resolution in the amount of $96,335 for FY 2014-2015 federal and state grant expenditures. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

g.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $1,106,900 for FY 2015 nondiscretionary expenditures.

 
Approved, unanimously, to approve an appropriation resolution in the amount of $1,106,900 for FY 2015 nondiscretionary expenditures. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
