 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article IV, Section 98-215 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to expand a permit parking district on Forest Avenue to include 10302-10313.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article IV, Section 98-215 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to expand a permit parking district along Forest Avenue to include 12 additional houses (10302-10313).
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Cellco Partnership d/b/a Verizon Wireless, applicant, by Ryan Dear, agent, for  a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-782 (a) (1) to allow a telecommunications facility that exceeds 65 feet in height when mounted on top of the commercial building in C-2 Retail Commercial District and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District, on the premises known as 10555 Main Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-4-02-069.

 
Approved, unanimously, the request of Cellco Partnership d/b/a Verizon Wireless, applicant, by Ryan Dear, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-782 (a) (1) to allow a telecommunications facility that exceeds 65 feet in height when mounted on top of a commercial building (Fairfax Building) in C-2 Retail Commercial District and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District, on the premises known as 10555 Main Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-4-02-069, subject to the following conditions:
1.         Construction shall be in substantial conformance with the attached Special Use Permit   Plat (Site Plan) dated March 6, 2014 and revised through May 4, 2015.
2.         Based on the RF Safety Report provided with the Special Use Permit application, warning signage shall be provided on the inside of the door providing roof access.
3.         Prior to issuance of permits, the applicant shall submit design to the Board of Architectural Review for approval. The design shall reflect similar design and finish of the existing penthouse.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Christopher Customs, LLC, by W. Craig Havenner, agent, for a Subdivision Ordinance Variance to not provide the road widening, curb, gutter and sidewalk required by Sections 86-7 and 86-8 in front of the premises known as 10400 Stratford Avenue and more particularly described as tax map parcels 57-2-02-054, 57-2-02-055, 57-2-02-056 and 57-2-02-057.

 
Approved, 4-1, the request of Christopher Customs, LLC, with conditions, for variances from Sections 86-7, 86-8(b), 86-8(c) and 86-8(d) of the City of Fairfax Subdivision Ordinance for the Sherwood consolidation and re-subdivision of parcels 57-2-02-054, 57-2-02-055, 57-2-02-056, and 57-2-02-057 with the following condition:
  1. The Applicant shall construct a pedestrian walkway along the Stratford Avenue frontage of the subject property as determined in consultation with the Department of Public Works.
(In favor, Councilmembers DeMarco, Loftus, Miller, and Schmidt; opposed, Councilman Meyer.)(Councilman Greenfield was absent from the vote.)
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of the City of Fairfax, Owner, and Robert Sisson, City Manager and Agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-512(17) to allow a child care center, and for a Special Exception pursuant to City Code Section 110-158 for a parking reduction, in the R-3 Residential District on the premises known as Green Acres located at 4401 Sideburn Road and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-4-01-008.

 
Approved, unanimously, the special use permit request of the City of Fairfax, Owner, and City Manager Robert Sisson, Agent, to allow a child care center for the former Green Acres School facility located in the R-3 Residential District at 4401 Sideburn Road and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-4-001-008, subject to the following conditions:
1.         The operation of the site’s child care center shall be consistent with the documentation contained herewith or as may be modified by its lease.
2.         The fenced outdoor play area shall be in compliance with the provisions of the City Code.
3.         The Director of Parks and Recreation shall schedule all use of the site so that parking demand does not exceed the 131 parking spaces provided at this site.Approved, unanimously, the special exception request of the City of Fairfax, Owner, and City Manager Robert Sisson, Agent, to allow a reduction in required parking for the former Green Acres School facility located in the R-3 Residential District at 4401 Sideburn Road and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-4-001-008, subject to the following conditions:
1.         The operation of the site’s child care center shall be consistent with the documentation contained herewith or as may be modified by its lease.
2.         The fenced outdoor play area shall be in compliance with the provisions of the City Code.
3.         The Director of Parks and Recreation shall schedule all use of the site so that parking demand does not exceed the 131 parking spaces provided at this site.
 
 

e.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease for a term beginning July 1, 2015 and ending June 30, 2017 by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia, and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc., for approximately 7,364 square feet of space in the former Green Acres Elementary School, located at 4401 Sideburn Road.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a deed of lease by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc. for premises located at 4401 Sideburn Road (a portion of the former Green Acres Elementary School).
 
 

f.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 90, Article II, Division 1, Section 90-31 and Article III, Section 90-167, pertaining to interest charged on delinquent personal property and real estate taxes.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 90, Article II, Division 1, Section 90-31(b)(2) and Article III, Section 90-167, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to interest charged on delinquent real estate taxes and personal property taxes.
