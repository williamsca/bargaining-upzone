                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 14, 2016 
 
 1.  Call the meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember Loftus).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance led by Daisy Troop 6902.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation acknowledging the month of June as Childhood Cancer Awareness Month.

 

b.

Presentation of a proclamation congratulating the Central Fairfax Optimist Club on their 30th Anniversary.

 

c.

Presentation of a certificate of appreciation to Michael Breslin for his service as the student representative on the Parks and Recreation Advisory Board.

 

d.

Presentation of a proclamation in recognition of Lynn Barbour’s retirement from the city as its IT Director and for his 32-years of service with the City.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of the revised City of Fairfax and Fairfax City School Board Agreement for Use of City Schools.

 

b.

Consideration of an award of contract to Hammel, Green, & Abrahamson, Inc. in an amount not to exceed $400,000 for the design and bidding services for the Police Firearms Training Center.

 

c.

Introduction of an ordinance amending Chapter 14, Article III Section 14-134, 14-134.1 and 14-136.1 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia as it pertains to home child care facility standards.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolutions for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater, Cable, Old Town, and Transportation Tax Funds for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2016.

 

Approved, unanimously, appropriation resolutions for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater, Cable, Old Town, and Transportation Tax Funds for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2016. (Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $321,682 for Fiscal Year 2015-2016 Federal, State and other Grant Expenditures.

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $321,682 for Fiscal Year 2015-2016 Federal, State and other Grant Expenditures.(Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $936,948 for FY 2016 nondiscretionary expenditures.

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $936,948 for FY 2016 nondiscretionary expenditures.(Councilmember Schmidt was absent.)
 
 

THE FOLLOWING FOUR (4) PUBLIC HEARINGS RELATED TO THE ZONING ORDINANCE REWRITE WERE ORIGINALLY SET FOR HEARING ON JUNE 14, 2016. 
ON MAY 24, 2016 THE CITY COUNCIL DEFERRED THE FOUR (4) PUBLIC HEARINGS 
TO JULY 12, 2016.

 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on a zoning map amendment of multiple properties in an existing development from the R-T6 Townhouse District to the RT Residential Townhouse District, with a density of up to twelve dwelling units per acre, on the land known as 9600-9630 Ridge Avenue, designated Low-Medium Density Residential with a density of 4.01 to 7.99 dwelling units per acre in the Comprehensive Plan, along with open space parcels, designated Open Space - Conservation in the Comprehensive Plan, otherwise known as tax map parcels 48-3-08-06-002 through 015 and 48-3-08-06-000 A and B.

 

e.

Public hearing and Council action on a zoning text amendment to amend the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 (Zoning) to repeal the current chapter in its entirety and readopt the chapter with significant revisions to all articles and sections contained therein, including, but not limited to: zoning districts and regulations, site development standards, development review procedures, nonconformities, enforcement and penalties, and definitions.

 

f.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 86 (Subdivisions) to repeal the current chapter in its entirety and readopt the chapter with significant revisions to all articles and sections contained therein, including, but not limited to: administrative procedures, design and improvements, enforcement and penalties, and definitions.

 

g.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 7 (Domestic Beekeeping) and Chapter 38, Article III, Section 38-31 (Environment) pertaining to the keeping of honey bees.

 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.  None.
