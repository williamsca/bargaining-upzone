Introduction of an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 90, Article II, Division 1, Section 90-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax used for or zoned for commercial and industrial purposes, and to establish a separate tax rate on such real property effective as of January 1, 2016, revenues from which would be used exclusively for transportation purposes that benefit the City of Fairfax.  (Public hearing April 27, 2016.)

 

f.

Introduction of an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 102, Article II, Section 102-31 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to increase the Wastewater Utility rate by 10% for Fiscal Year 2016-2017.  (Public hearing April 27, 2016.)

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda item. (Councilman DeMarco was absent.)
