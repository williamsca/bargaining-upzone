a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Luby’s Fuddruckers Restaurants, LLC, by David E. Hawkins, Agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(6) to allow the enlargement, extension, reconstruction or structural alteration of nonconforming uses, buildings, lots, and/or locations in the C-2 district, and associated Special Exceptions including modification of the required front and side yard requirements pursuant to City Code Section 110-783(6), in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and Highway Corridor Overlay District on the land known as 3575 Chain Bridge Road and more particularly described as tax maps 57-2-02-024 A and B.

 

Approved, 4-3, the request of Luby’s Fuddruckers Restaurants, LLC, by David E. Hawkins, agent, for a special use permit to allow structural alteration of an existing non-conforming site and building in the C-2 Retail Commercial District pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(6), on the premises known as 3575 Chain Bridge Road, and more particularly described as tax map parcels 57-2-2-24a and 57-2-2-24b. (In favor: Mayor Silverthorne, and Councilmembers Greenfield, Loftus, and Schmidt; opposed: Councilmembers DeMarco, Meyer, and Miller.)

 

Approved, 4-3, the request of Luby’s Fuddruckers Restaurants, LLC, by David E. Hawkins, agent, to reduce the minimum front and side yard requirements pursuant to Section 110-783(6); for development of the property located at on the premises known as 3575 Chain Bridge Road, and more particularly described as tax map parcels 57-2-2-24a and 57-2-2-24b. (In favor: Mayor Silverthorne, and Councilmembers Greenfield, Loftus, and Schmidt; opposed: Councilmembers DeMarco, Meyer, and Miller.)
