 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of AT&T, by Clare Liedquist, Agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(1) to allow a telecommunications facility that together with the structure on which it is mounted, exceeds 65 feet in height in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and the Old Town Fairfax Transition District on the land known as 10555 Main Street and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-4-02-069.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of AT&T, by Clare Liedquist, Agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-782(a)(1) to allow a telecommunications facility that together with the structure on which it is mounted, exceeds 65 feet in height in the C-2 Retail Commercial District and the Old Town Fairfax Transition District on the land known as 10555 Main Street and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-4-02-069, subject to the following conditions:

  1. Construction shall be in substantial conformance with the Special Use Permit Plans prepared by Entrex Communication Services, Inc. for AT&T – Judicial Drive, revised through February 22, 2016.
    2.  Additional screening or other minor deviations to the plans may be approved by the Director of Community Development and Planning but are subject to approval by the Board of Architectural Review and receipt of all required permits.

Approved 5-1, advertised $1.0815 as the maximum real estate tax rate for fiscal year 2016-17. (Councilmember Loftus opposed.)
