                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         July 12, 2016 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember Miller).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of the Mayor’s awards.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of the 2016-2018 City Council term proposed procedural and operational resolutions.

 

b.

Consideration of Homeowners’ Association Covenants for Cobbs Grove Subdivision.

 

c.

Consideration of a request by the American Diabetes Association for an additional road race and event to be held in FY17, which requires a street closure.

 

d.

Consideration of a resolution to authorize the issuance and sale of one or more series of General Obligation Refunding Bonds in the maximum principal amount of $23,000,000, provided certain market conditions are met, in accordance with the recommendations of the City’s Financial Advisor.

 

e.

Consideration of the 2016-2018 City Council term proposed resolution adopting City Council goals.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items. (Councilmember Schmidt abstained from #7d.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a zoning map amendment of multiple properties in an existing development from the R-T6 Townhouse District to the RT Residential Townhouse District on the land known as 9600-9630 Ridge Avenue, along with open space parcels, otherwise known as tax map parcels 48-3-08-06-002 through 015 and 48-3-08-06-000 A and B. (MOTION TO WITHDRAW)

 
Approved, unanimously, to withdraw the zoning map amendment in conjunction with the repeal and readoption of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 (Zoning), the application of the City of Fairfax to rezone the subject property from R-T6 - Townhouse to RT Townhouse; on the land known as 9600 to 9630 Ridge Avenue and more particularly described as tax map parcels 48-3-08-06-001 through 015 and 48-3-08-06-000 A and B.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on proposed amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 (Zoning) to repeal the current chapter in its entirety and readopt the chapter with significant revisions to all articles and sections contained therein, including, but not limited to: zoning districts and regulations, site development standards, development review procedures, nonconformities, enforcement and penalties, and definitions.

 
Approved, unanimously, amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 110 (Zoning) to repeal the current chapter in its entirety and readopt the chapter with significant revisions to all articles and sections contained therein, including, but not limited to: zoning districts and regulations, site development standards, development review procedures, nonconformities, enforcement and penalties, and definitions. (Effective October 1, 2016.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on proposed amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 86 (Subdivisions) to repeal the current chapter in its entirety and readopt the chapter with significant revisions to all articles and sections contained therein, including, but not limited to: administrative procedures, design and improvements, enforcement and penalties, and definitions.

 

Approved, unanimously, amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 86 (Subdivisions) to repeal the current chapter in its entirety and readopt the chapter with significant revisions to all articles and sections contained therein, including, but not limited to: administrative procedures, design and improvements, enforcement and penalties, and definitions. (Effective October 1, 2016.)
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on proposed amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 7 (Domestic Beekeeping) and Chapter 38, Article III, Section 38-31 (Environment) pertaining to the keeping of honey bees.

 

Approved, unanimously, amendments to the Code of the City of Fairfax, Chapter 7 (Domestic Beekeeping) and Chapter 38, Article III, Section 38-31 (Environment) pertaining to the keeping of honey bees. (Effective October 1, 2016.)
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of a Resolution of City Funding Commitment for Northfax Intersection and Drainage Improvements Project.

 

Approved, unanimously, a resolution of City Funding Commitment for Northfax Intersection and Drainage Improvements Project.
 
 

b.

Consideration of an award of Construction Contract to Branch Highways Inc. for the Northfax Intersection and Drainage Improvements Project.

 

Approved, unanimously, a construction contract to Branch Highways Inc. for the Northfax Intersection and Drainage Improvements Project.
