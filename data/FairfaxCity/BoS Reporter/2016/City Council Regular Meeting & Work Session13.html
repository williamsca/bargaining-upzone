                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         November 8, 2016 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember DeMarco).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation acknowledging the 90th Birthday of Armistice Turtora.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between the City of Fairfax and the Northern Virginia Soil and Water Conservation District.

 

b.

Consideration of a resolution to endorse the City’s submission to the Virginia Department of Transportation for FY 18-22/23 Smart Scale Funding.

 

c.

Consideration of a resolution to endorse the City’s submission to the Virginia Department of Transportation for FY 18 Revenue Sharing Funding.

 

d.

Consideration of a resolution approving the City’s submission to the Northern Virginia Transportation Authority for RSTP/CMAQ funding.

 

e.

Consideration of an award of contract to Sagres Construction Corporation in an estimated amount of $167,763 for the construction of the University Drive sidewalk project at Armstrong Street.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Article III, Chapter 38, Sections 38-31, 38-34, 38-37 of the code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to public health and safety menaces.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Article III, Chapter 38, Sections 38-31, 38-34, 38-37, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to public health or safety menaces.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 66 of the Code of the City of Fairfax as it pertains to personnel.

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 66 of the Code of the City of Fairfax as it pertains to personnel.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $694,449 for funding awarded to the City through Virginia Department of Transportation’s FY17 Primary Extension Paving Program.

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $694,449 for funding awarded to the City through Virginia Department of Transportation's FY17 Primary Extension Paving Program.
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on City Council Proposed Budget Guidelines for FY 17-18 budget process.

 

Approved, unanimously, the proposed budget guidelines to be used in the preparation of the Fiscal Year 2018 budget.
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of the City of Fairfax’s 2017 Legislative Program.

 

Approved, unanimously, the City of Fairfax FY17 Legislative Program.
 
 
 
 10.  Approval of minutes.

a.

October 25, 2016 regular meeting.

 
 
 11.  Convene a closed meeting for consultation with legal counsel and appointments to Boards and Commissions.
 
 12.  Actions out of Closed Meeting/Appointments to Boards and Commissions.
  • Reappointed Hildie Carney to Historic Fairfax City, Inc. for a five-year term extending through December 31, 2021.
  • Appointed Kelly McLaughlan to the Economic Development Authority for the remainder of a four-year term extending through November 1, 2019.
  • Appointed Sean Mallon to the Economic Development Authority for the remainder of a four-year term extending through October 23, 2016, with an extension of a four-year term extending through October 23, 2020.
 
 
 
 13.  Council Comments.
 
 14.  Adjournment.
 
 15.  WORK SESSION.

1.

Discussion and follow-up on the University Drive Extension Program.

 

2.

Discussion of City’s Compliance with the American Disabilities Act (ADA).
