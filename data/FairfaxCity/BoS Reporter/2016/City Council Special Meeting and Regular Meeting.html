a.

Consideration of a resolution that (a) approves the lease/purchase of vehicles and equipment with US Bancorp Government Leasing and Financing, Inc., and (b) approves the amendment of the existing master lease with the financing lessor for this borrowing.

 

b.

Consideration of a request from a non-resident of the city to purchase a cemetery plot.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items. (Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Tots n Us Children’s Academy, by Lajwanthi Wadhwa, applicant, for consideration of a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-482 to allow a Child Care Center in the R-2 zoning district on the premises known as 3711 Jermantown Rd and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel(s) 47-3-((04))-013 A and 47-3-((04))-014 A.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Tots n Us Children’s Academy by Lajwanthi Wadhwa, applicant, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Sections 110-5(c) and 110-366(2) to allow a child care center on the property located at 3711 Jermantown Road, and more particularly described as tax map parcels 47-3-((04))-013-A and 47-3-((004))-014-A, subject to the following conditions:

  1. All operations shall be consistent with the descriptions included in the applicant’s statement of             support as provided in Attachment 3.
  2. The enrollment shall be limited to 74 children.
  3. The applicant shall obtain and maintain any license(s) required by the state to operate.
  4. The outdoor play areas shall not be in use between the hours of 7:00 p.m. and 8:00 a.m.
  5. The special use permit will not be transferable upon changing ownership.

(Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 14, Article III Section 14-134, 14-134.1 and 14-136.1 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia as it pertains to home child care facility standards.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 14, Article III Section 14-134, 14-134.1 and 14-136.1 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia as it pertains to home child care facility standards. (Councilmember Loftus was absent.)
