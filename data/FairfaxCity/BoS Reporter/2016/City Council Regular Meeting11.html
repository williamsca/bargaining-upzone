Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a License Agreement with Clarks Corner Homeowners Association, Inc. to install and to continue to maintain certain installed landscaping improvements within a portion of the City’s right-of-way for a period of five (5) years.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a License Agreement with Clarks Corner Homeowners Association, Inc. to install and to continue to maintain certain installed landscaping improvements within a portion of the City’s right-of-way for a period of five (5) years.
