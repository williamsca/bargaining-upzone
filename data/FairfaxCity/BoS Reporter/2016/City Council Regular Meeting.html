Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article I of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to add a new section pertaining to a bicycle helmet law for children.

 
Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article I of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, to add a new section 98-23, pertaining to the use of a protective helmet while operating a bicycle.
