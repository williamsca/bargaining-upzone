 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on the proposed project which involves constructing a new continuous sidewalk on the west side of Chain Bridge Road from Rust Hill Place to just north of North Street. The project will also eliminate existing erosion/drainage problems and roadside hazards within the project limits.

 

Approved, 4-2, to direct staff to consider changes to the proposed project consistent with the comments received from the City Council and the public, seek additional project funding, finalize the plans and proceed to right-of-way acquisition and construction. (Councilmembers Greenfield and Schmidt opposed.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 14, Article II, Division 1, Section 14-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, as it pertains to license taxes applicable to persons having gross receipts of $50,000 or less.

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 14, Article II, Division 1, Section 14-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, as it pertains to license taxes applicable to persons having gross receipts of $50,000 or less.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $56,000 for the addition of Transportation Alternatives Program to the budget and the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund for Metro to Mason University Drive Road Diet Project.

 
Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $56,000 for the addition of Transportation Alternatives Program to the budget and the transfer of NVTA 30% monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund for Metro to Mason University Drive Road Diet Project.
