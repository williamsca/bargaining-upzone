b.

Introduction of an appropriation resolution in the amount of $79,260 to fund the additional scope of work for the City Cemetery columbarium project.

8.

Public hearings.

 

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $184,400 to purchase an Electronic Summons System for the Police Department.

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $184,400 to fund the purchase of an Electronic Summons System for the Police Department.
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 14 Article III, Sections 14-131 through 14-140 regarding minimum home child care facility standards.

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending and reenacting Chapter 14, Article III, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to home child care facility standards.
 
 

9.

Items not requiring a public hearing.

 

a.

Discussion and consideration of a resolution to endorse the City’s submission to the Virginia Department of Transportation for FY 24/25 Smart Scale Funding.

 

Approved, 5-1, a resolution to endorse the City’s submission to the Virginia Department of Transportation for FY 24/25 Smart Scale Funding for the three projects outlined in the resolution and pursuant to the discussion at the July 10, 2018 City Council meeting. (Councilmember Yi opposed.)

Discussion and consideration of City Council 2018-2020 term policies and procedures.

 

No Council action taken.
