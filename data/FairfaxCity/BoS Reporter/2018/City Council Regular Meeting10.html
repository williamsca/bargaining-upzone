                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 26, 2018 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember Schmidt).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentations to Councilmember Eleanor Schmidt.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration to authorize the City Manager to execute a Third Amendment to the Capital Funding Agreement with the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA).

 

b.

Introduction of a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $120,000 to purchase an Electronic Summons System for the Police Department.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public Hearing and Council action on the City of Fairfax Van Dyck Park Master Plan.

 

Approved, unanimously, the Van Dyck Park Master Plan. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action of an ordinance amending and reenacting Chapter 102 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to utilities (and specifically the provisions relating to water and wastewater).

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending and reenacting Chapter 102 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to utilities. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 98, Article IV, Division 3 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to residential parking permits.

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending and reenacting Chapter 98, Article IV, Division 3, of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to residential parking permits. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 

d.

Public Hearing and Council action of a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $250,000 to add additional RSTP funds to the Capital Fund to fully fund the Roberts Road Sidewalk Project.

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation resolution in the amount of $250,000 to fund construction of the Roberts Road Sidewalk Project. (Councilmember Passey was absent.)
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.  None.
 
 10.  Approval of minutes. 

a.

June 12, 2018 Regular Meeting.

 
 
 11.  Recess the regular meeting to go into a work session.

a.

Discussion on the Funding Update and Smart Scale applications.

 
 
 13.  Reconvene the regular meeting.
 
 14.  Council Comments.
 
 15.  Closed meeting to discuss personnel matters.
 
 16.  Adjournment.
