(THE APPLICANT HAS REQUESTED DEFERRAL.)  Public hearing and Council action on a request from Paradigm Development Company, applicant, by Matthew G. Roberts, Esq., Agent/Attorney, for consideration of a Planned Development Review pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.6, a Special Exception to allow a sidewalk width of less than 10 feet within the Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.7.3.D and a Special Exception to allow a front yard width that exceeds 10 feet within the Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.7.3.C.3, to allow development of up to 43 residential townhouse units on the premises known as 4131 Chain Bridge Road and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel(s) 57-4-02-040.

 
Deferred, unanimously, the public hearing and consideration of this matter to the regular City Council meeting on June 12, 2018. He further moved that, to the extent the applicant makes material changes to the application as currently presented, in the determination of City staff, that this matter be referred back to the Planning Commission for appropriate action and recommendation, without the need for further action by City Council to initiate such referral.
