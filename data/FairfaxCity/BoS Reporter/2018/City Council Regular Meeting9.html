b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request by the applicant, Sarah Tarvid, for Special Use Permits pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B and 110-3.5.5 to allow a school and day camp (as an accessory) at Truro Anglican Church, a split-zoned property in the RM, Residential Medium and CR, Commercial Retail zoning and Old Town Fairfax Historic Overlay districts on the premises known as 10520 Main Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-2-02-157.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Sarah Tarvid, applicant, for special use permits pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1b and 110-3.5.5 to allow a school and day camp (as an accessory) at Truro Church, a split-zoned property in the RM, Residential Medium and CR, Commercial Retail Zoning and Old Town Fairfax Historic Overlay Districts on the premises known as 10520 Main Street, and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-2-02-157, subject to the following conditions:

1. The maximum combined number of students enrolled at the preschool and elementary school shall not exceed 250. Operation of the day camps including the number of students enrolled shall be consistent with information provided in the approved application and plans.

2. The applicant shall follow the approved floorplans showing the designated classrooms for preschool, elementary school classes and day camp programs provided with the special use permit application.

3. The applicant shall provide and maintain where necessary, way finding signage to preschool and elementary school parking area and entrance, subject to approval by zoning.

4. A drop off arrangement shall not be allowed; cars must be parked and students walked into school.

5. The special use permits shall be valid for three years and expire on June 12, 2021.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from City of Fairfax, applicant, by Cathy Salgado, Agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.B to allow a parks and open areas use in the Residential High zoning district at the site of the former Westmore Elementary School on the premises known as 11000 Berry Street, and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-1-01-009.

 
Approved, unanimously, the request of City of Fairfax by Cathy Salgado, Director of Parks & Recreation, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.b to allow a parks and open areas use in the RH Residential High Zoning District for the premises known as 11000 Berry Street and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-01-009 subject to the following conditions:

1. The proposed site modifications shall conform to the plans and review materials received by staff on March 26, 2018 and modified through the date of the public hearing.

2. Any expansions of the facility, new facilities, or changes to the site shall require an amendment to this Special Use Permit to be reviewed and approved by City Council.

 Approved, unanimously, the request of the City of Fairfax by Cathy Salgado, Director of Parks and Recreation, for a major Certificate of Appropriateness pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.5 to construct a dog park and make site improvements on the premises known as 11000 Berry Street and more particularly described as tax map parcel 57-1-01-009, with the following condition:

1. The proposed construction, materials, and landscaping shall conform to the plans and review materials received by staff on March 26, 2018 and modified through the date of the public hearing.
