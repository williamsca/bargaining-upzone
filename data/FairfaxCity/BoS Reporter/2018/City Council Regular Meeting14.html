Public hearing and Council action on a request from Chosen Presbyterian Church, applicant, by Ms. Carol Kim and Mr. Chong Kim, agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B for a Religious Institution (Chosen Presbyterian Church) in the CR Commercial Retail zoning district on the premises known as 3843 Plaza Drive and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 57-2-25-005.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request from Chosen Presbyterian Church, applicant, by Ms. Carol Kim and Mr. Chong Kim, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B for a religious institution use (Chosen Presbyterian Church) in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District on the premises known as 3843 Plaza Drive and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcels 57-2-25-005, 57-2-25-001, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The applicant will submit a shared parking agreement with the condominium association for approval to the Zoning Administrator prior to obtaining a use & occupancy permit.
  2. The church will hold worship and assembly only at following times:
  • Monday - Friday from 5a.m. to 7a.m. and 7p.m. to 12a.m.(maximum 40 attendees)
  • Saturday from 5a.m. to 7a.m. and from 4p.m. to 10p.m. (maximum 40 attendees)
  • Sunday from 8a.m. to 7p.m. (maximum 82 attendees)
  • Special Holidays (Good Friday, Christmas Eve, and New Year ’s Eve from 7p.m. to 12a.m. (maximum 82 attendees).
  1. Noise levels shall comply with operational performance standards for noise in the Zoning Ordinance for commercial uses.
  2. Any outdoor activity shall be limited to Sunday from 8a.m. to 7p.m. subject to provisions within the zoning ordinance.
