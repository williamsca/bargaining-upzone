 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Banfield Pet Hospital for a Special Use Permit to allow an animal care facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District located at 9516 Main Street or more particularly described as tax map parcel 58-3-02-028 A.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request the request of Medical Management International, Inc., dba Banfield Pet Hospital, applicant, by Stephen Miller, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1b to allow an animal care facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 9516 Main Street and more particularly described as tax map parcel 58-3-02-028 a, subject to the following conditions:

  1. Operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and plans received July 12, 2018 and August 29, 2018.
  2. The applicant shall limit the hours of operation to seven days a week, 7:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m.
  3. The applicant shall work with the property owner to ensure that the three 15-minute spaces emain to allow easy access to drop off/pick up animals.
  4. The applicant shall incorporate sound mitigating measures into the design as   shown on the submitted plans and details, for the proposed veterinarian hospital, to prevent noise levels, if any, from creating any adverse impact on adjacent commercial establishments or nearby residential properties.
  5. The applicant shall ensure all activities associated with the care and treatment of animals shall occur within the building.
  6. The applicant shall provide for proper disposal of medical waste and euthanized animals as required by local, state and federal regulations. All storage of waste shall be inside the facility.
  7. The applicant shall monitor the shopping center premises to ensure that no animal waste accumulation occurs.
  8. All local, state and federal licenses and permits shall be maintained and kept    current.
  9. The Special Use Permit shall be valid for a period of two years. Prior to expiration, the applicant shall apply for renewal of the Special Use Permit application or it shall be deemed null and void.

(Councilmember DeMarco was absent.)
