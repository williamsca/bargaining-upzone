 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of the maximum real estate tax rate for FY19 to be advertised. 
(This action sets the maximum real estate tax rate that can be adopted as part of the FY19 budget. This action does not set the actual real estate tax rate for FY19.)

 

Approved, unanimously, to advertise a proposed 2018 real estate tax rate of $1.0825 per $100 of assessed value. (Councilman Greenfield was absent.)
 
 

b.

Introduction of a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution in the amount of $475,000 for preliminary transportation design and engineering for four (4) Smart Scale applications.

 

Approved, unanimously, to introduce an appropriation resolution in the amount of $475,000, transferring monies from the Transportation Tax Fund to the Capital Fund in order to fund transportation planning and engineering tasks for proposed Smart Scale Projects and waive the first reading and set a public hearing for March 27, 2018. (Councilman Greenfield was absent.)
 
 

c.

Consideration of a Task Order in the amount of $219,173 to Volkert for Engineering Services for the Jermantown Road Corridor Improvements Project.

 

Approved, unanimously, to authorize the City Manager to approve the fee proposal for a Task Order for Volkert for engineering services for the Jermantown Road Corridor Improvements Project in an estimated amount of $219,173. (Councilman Greenfield was absent.)
 
 

d.

Consideration of a Task Order in the amount of $205,000 to JMT for Engineering Services for the Eaton Place/Chain Bridge Road Intersection Project.

 

Approved, unanimously, to authorize the City Manager to approve the fee proposal for a Task Order for JMT for engineering services for the Eaton Place/Chain Bridge Road Intersection Improvements Project in an estimated amount of $205,000. (Councilman Greenfield was absent.)
