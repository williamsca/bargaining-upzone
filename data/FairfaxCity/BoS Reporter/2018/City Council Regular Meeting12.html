a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Room Escape DC, LLC, applicant, by Javier Arencibia, agent, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1B for an indoor recreation use (Room Escape DC) in the CR Commercial Retail zoning district and Old Town Fairfax Transition overlay districts on the premises known as 3937, 3939, 3947 and 3949 3949 A University Drive and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcels 57-2-22-013,017, 019 and 016.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request from Room Escape DC, LLC, applicant, by Javier Arencibia, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1b for an indoor recreation use (Room Escape DC) in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Old Town Fairfax Transition Overlay District on the premises known as 3937, 3939, 3947 and 3949 3949 A University Drive and more particularly described as tax map parcels 57-2-22-013,017, 019 and 016, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The business shall operate in accordance with the Statement of Justification and floor plans submitted with the Special Use Permit application, except that interior modifications may be allowed as long as the capacity and number of gaming rooms does not increase and remain   on the basement level.
  2. The hours of operation shall be seven days a week, 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 a.m.
  3. Noise levels shall comply with operational performance standards for noise in the Zoning Ordinance for commercial uses.
  4. Participants under the age of 16 shall be accompanied by a parent or guardian chaperone.
  5. All activities shall occur indoors and be limited to the leased areas identified on the approved floor plan.
  6. Access between suites 3939A and 3949A shall be permitted only if allowed by the Fire Marshal.

(Councilmembers DeMarco and Stehle were absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an Appropriation Resolution in the maximum amount of $12,000 to American Legion Post 177 ($10,000) and Yellow Ribbons United ($2,000).

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the total maximum amount of $12,000 for a monetary contribution to American Legion Post 177 ($10,000) and Yellow Ribbons United ($2,000) made available pro rata on approximately a quarterly basis throughout Councilmember Yi’s term in office. (Councilmembers DeMarco and Stehle were absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $79,260 to fund the additional scope of work for the City Cemetery columbarium project.

 

Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the additional amount of $79,260 to fund the City Cemetery Columbarium Project, for the fiscal year, beginning July 1, 2018. (Councilmembers DeMarco and Stehle were absent.)

Approved, unanimously, a resolution to repeal the Design Guidelines for the Old Town Fairfax Historic and Transition Districts and the Community Appearance Plan, and to adopt the City of Fairfax Design Guidelines draft dated July 19, 2018. (Councilmembers DeMarco and Stehle were absent.)
