Introduction of an ordinance amending Chapter 54 (Offenses and Miscellaneous Provisions) of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to:  (i) repeal Article I, Section 54-4 (relating to Beaver Dam Creek Reservoir and Goose Creek Reservoir); (ii) amend Article III, Division 1, Section 54-61 and 54-62 (relating to petit larceny and concealing of merchandise and altering price tags); and (iii) amend Article IV, Division 2, Sections 54-141 and 54-142 (relating to bad checks).

16.

Actions out of Closed Meeting/Appointments to Boards and Commissions.

 
  • Appointed Thoria Hassan to the Community Appearance Committee for the remainder of a three-year term extending through November 20, 2020.
  • Reappointed Gail Wade to the George Mason University Fairfax Campus Advisory Board for a two-year term extending through January 11, 2021.
  • Reappointed Zinta Rodgers-Rickert to the Parks and Recreation Advisory Board for a three-year term extending through September 1, 2021.
  • Authorized and approved the settlement, on the terms and conditions outlined by the City Attorney in closed meeting, of Army Navy Country Club v. City of Fairfax (Fairfax County Circuit Court Case No. cl2015-17941) (tax assessment appeal matter) and authorized the City Attorney to finalize the negotiations and any settlement agreement in this matter and to authorize the City Attorney, the Acting City Manager and the Mayor, as applicable, to execute any documentation necessary to finalize the settlement and to conclude this matter.
  • Authorized and approved the settlement, on the terms and conditions outlined by the City Attorney in closed meeting, of City of Fairfax v. Mckay Family Investments (currently pending in the Fairfax County Circuit Court), relating to the acquisition of certain right of way from the defendant as part of the Northfax Project and authorized the City Attorney to finalize the negotiations and any settlement agreement in this matter and to authorize the City Attorney, the City Manager and the Mayor, as applicable, to execute any documentation necessary to finalize the settlement and to conclude the court case.
