Public hearings.

 

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request by Colleen Bilowus, owner, for renewal of a Special Use Permit, pursuant to City Code Section 110-482(16) to allow a home daycare for a maximum of 12 children, on the premises known as 10319 Wood Road and more particularly described as Tax Map 56-2-06-10-003.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Colleen Bilowus, Owner, for renewal of a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow a family home daycare for a maximum of twelve (12) children in the RM Zoning District on the premises known as 10319 Wood Road and more particularly described as tax map parcel 56-2-06-10-003, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The applicant shall care for a maximum of twelve (12) children;
  2. Unless the applicant can clearly demonstrate to Zoning Administration, prior to its approval, that an onsite or offsite parking arrangement would support additional employees, the employees allowed to work at the home daycare shall be limited to those would reside on the premises;
  3. During the hours of operation, two driveway parking spaces shall be available for dropping off or picking up children;
  4. No signage pertaining to the child care business shall be permitted on the premises;
  5. The outdoor play area shall not be used for playing prior to 8:00 AM;
  6. There shall be no modifications to the exterior of the building which detract from the residential character of the site;
  7. The Special Use Permit shall be valid only for the applicant and shall not be transferrable to any other person; and
  8. The applicant shall obtain and maintain all licenses required by the state to operate.

Public hearing and Council action on a supplemental appropriation resolution to add $14M into the City budget for the George Snyder Trail project.
