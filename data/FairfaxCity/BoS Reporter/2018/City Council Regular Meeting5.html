 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public Hearing and Council action on a Supplemental Appropriation Resolution for $60,000 in Local C&I funding for the Route 123 Bikeshare Feasibility Study.

 

Approved, unanimously, a supplemental appropriation resolution in an amount of $60,000 to fund the Route 123 Bike Share Feasibility Study. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public Hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending the public rights-of-way franchise granted to Virginia Electric and Power Company d/b/a Dominion Energy Virginia in Ordinance No. 2013-10, enacted on May 14, 2013 to address certain obligations of the city and franchisee with respect to undergrounding of electric facilities.

 

Approved, unanimously, to enact an ordinance amending the existing franchise agreement dated May 13, 2018, between the City of Fairfax and Virginia Electric and Power Company d/b/a Dominion Energy Virginia and authorize the Mayor to execute the ordinance, once finalized with any modifications as approved by the City Attorney. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of a resolution authorizing the city manager to enter into funding agreement with George Mason University and Fairfax County to fund a bikeshare feasibility study.

 

Approved, unanimously, to approve a resolution to authorize the City Manager to enter into a funding agreement with Fairfax County and George Mason University for the Route 123 Bike Share Feasibility Study provided that the agreement and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
 
 

b.

Consideration of an award of contract to Toole Design Group (TDG) in the amount of $55,646 for conducting the Route 123 Bike Share Study.

 

Approved, unanimously, to authorize the City Manager to enter into a contract with Toole Design Group to conduct the Route 123 Bike Share Feasibility Study in an estimated amount of $55,646 provided that the contract and any amendments are acceptable to the City Manager and approved as to form by the City Attorney. (Councilmember Miller was absent.)
