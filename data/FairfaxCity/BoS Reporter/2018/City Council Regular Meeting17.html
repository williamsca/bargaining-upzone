Public hearing and Council action on a request of 9571 Fairfax Boulevard, LLC, applicant, by Stuart D. Schooler, agent, for Special Exceptions pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.17 to: modify the required minimum front yard setback; modify the required minimum interior side yard setback; provide relief from the requirement to provide a sidewalk along a street; provide relief from meeting landscape strip and street tree requirements along streets; modify the terminal landscape islands minimum size and dimension requirements; and provide relief from the requirement of a parking space and planting island location; and for a Certificate of Appropriateness for architecture and landscaping pursuant to City Code Section 110-6.5 in order to develop a general retail establishment in the CG Commercial General Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 9571 Fairfax Boulevard and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 48-3-06-041.

 

Denied, 4-1, the request by 9571 Fairfax Boulevard, LLC, applicant, by Stuart D. Schooler, agent, for special exceptions to:

  1. Modify the required minimum front yard setback;
  2. Modify the required minimum interior side yard setback;
  3. Provide relief from the requirement to provide a sidewalk along a street;
  4. Provide relief from meeting landscape strip and street tree requirements along streets;
  5. Modify the terminal landscape islands minimum size and dimension requirements; and
  6. Provide relief from the requirement of a parking space and planting island location in order to develop a general retail establishment in the CG Commercial General Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District on the premises known as 9571 Fairfax Boulevard and more particularly described as tax map parcel 48-3-06-041, for the following reason:
  • The proposal does not ensure the same general level of land use compatibility as the otherwise applicable standards.

(Councilmember Stehle opposed and Councilmember Lim was absent.)

 Denied, 4-1, the request by Stuart D. Schooler, agent, on behalf of 9571 Fairfax Boulevard, LLC, applicant, regarding case number BAR-18-00405, for a certificate of appropriateness, in accordance with Section 110-6.5.6.b of the City Code, on the premises known as 9571 Fairfax Boulevard and more particularly described as tax map parcel 48-3-06-041. (Councilmember Stehle opposed and Councilmember Lim was absent.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Maria Melendez De Reyes, applicant, by Lauren Kuiper, agent, for consideration of a renewal of Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5C to allow a Family Home Daycare with up to 12 children maximum in the RH, Residential High Zoning District on the premises known as 3633 Heritage Lane and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 58-1-15-012.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Maria Melendez De Reyes, applicant, by Lauren Kuiper, agent, for consideration of a renewal of a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5c to allow a family home daycare with up to 12 children maximum in the RH, Residential High Zoning District on the premises known as 3633 Heritage Lane and more particularly described as tax map parcel 58-1-15-012, subject to the following conditions:

  1. The applicant shall care for a maximum of up to twelve children;
  2. The hours of operation shall be 7:00 am to 5:30 pm, Monday through Friday;
  3. No more than two employees, exclusive of the applicant, shall be permitted during the hours of childcare use;
  4. Two of the driveway spaces shall be maintained for parking of two automobiles for   the residential use, and two spaces shall be available for drop-off and pick-up during the hours of operation for the home day care;
  5. No signage pertaining to the child care business shall be permitted on the premises;
  6. The outdoor play area shall not be used for playing prior to 8:00 AM;
  7. There shall be no modifications to the exterior of the building which detract from the residential character of the site;
  8. The special use permit shall be valid only for the applicant and shall not be transferrable to any other person; and
  9. The applicant shall maintain all licenses required by the state to operate.

(Councilmember Lim was absent.)
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an ordinance amending Chapter 54 (Offenses and Miscellaneous Provisions) of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to:  (i) repeal Article I, Section 54-4 (relating to Beaver Dam Creek Reservoir and Goose Creek Reservoir); (ii) amend Article III, Division 1, Section 54-61 and 54-62 (relating to petit larceny and concealing of merchandise and altering price tags); and (iii) amend Article IV, Division 2, Sections 54-141 and 54-142 (relating to bad checks).

 

Approved, unanimously, an ordinance amending Chapter 54 (Offenses and Miscellaneous Provisions) of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to: (i) repeal Article I, Section 54-4 (relating to Beaver Dam Creek Reservoir and Goose Creek Reservoir); (ii) amend Article III, Division 1, Section 54-61 and 54-62 (relating to petit larceny and concealing of merchandise and altering price tag); and (iii) amend Article IV, Division 2, Sections 54-141 and 54-142 (relating to bad checks). (Councilmember Lim was absent.)
