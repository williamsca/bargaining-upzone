 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Nosheen Iftikhar, applicant, for approval of a Special Use Permit, pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow a Family Home Daycare with more than 4 children in the RL, Residential Low Zoning District on the premises known as 10600 Howerton Ave and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 47-4-04-026.

 
Approved, unanimously that the City Council approve the request from Nosheen Iftikhar, applicant, for approval of a special use permit, pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow a family home daycare with more than 4 children in the RL, Residential Low Zoning District on the premises known as 10600 Howerton Avenue and more particularly described as tax map parcel 47-4-04-026, subject to the following conditions:

1. The operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and Plans. The Zoning Administrator shall be enabled to approve minor changes.
2. The outdoor play area shall not be used for playing prior to 8:00 a.m.
3. The driveway shall be maintained for parking of two automobiles for the residential use and two for the family home daycare use.
4. No signage pertaining to the childcare business shall be permitted on the premises.
5. There shall be no modifications to the exterior of the building which detract from the residential character of the site.
6. The applicant shall obtain and maintain all licenses required by the State to operate.
7. The special use permit shall be valid only for the applicant and shall not be transferrable to any other person.
