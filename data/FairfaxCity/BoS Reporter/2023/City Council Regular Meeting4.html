                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         March 14, 2023 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Moment of Silence
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance to the Flag.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation acknowledging March as Women’s History Month in the City of Fairfax.

 
 
 5.  General public comment (a maximum of three (3) minutes for any items not requiring a public hearing).
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
Approved, unanimously the adoption of the agenda.
 
 
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of the February 28, 2023 Special Meeting minutes.

 

b.

Consideration of the February 28, 2023 Regular Meeting minutes.

 

c.

Consideration to receive the list of delinquent taxes from the City Treasurer in compliance with State Code Section 58.1-3921, write off taxes in compliance with State Code Section 58.1-3940 and consideration to publish the list of delinquent taxes.

 

d.

Consideration of a resolution adopting Northern Virginia Hazard Mitigation Plan.

 
Approved, unanimously the consent agenda.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing on the proposed FY24 budget.

 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of the maximum real estate tax rate for fiscal year 2023-2024 to be advertised.

 
Approved, unanimously the City Council advertise a proposed real estate tax rate of $1.04 per $100 of assessed value.
 
 
 
 10.  Recess the regular meeting to go into a work session.

a.

Discussion and update on Economic Development Office Strategic Work-plan and Economic Development Authority Business Investment Initiatives and Functions.

 

b.

Discussion and update on the proposed design for Chain Bridge Road/Eaton place project.

 

c.

Discussion on the two-year Transportation program.

 

d.

Discussion and update on the stormwater utility.

 
 
 12.  Reconvene the regular meeting.
 
 13.  City Manager report out (if needed).
 
 14.  Council Comments and Committee reports out (if needed).
 
 15.  Closed meeting (if needed).
 
 16.  Adjournment.
