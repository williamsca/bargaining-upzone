City of Fairfax, Virginia 22030-3630

                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         May 2, 2023 
 
 1.  Call the special meeting to order.
 
 2.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing on an appropriation resolution for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater Utility, Cable, Old Town, Transportation tax, and the American Rescue Plan Act (ARPA) funds for the FY 2024 budget year. (Council consideration as agenda item 3).

 
 
 3.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 90, Article II, Division I, Section 90-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax used for or zoned for commercial and industrial purposes, and to establish a separate tax rate on such real property effective as of January 1, 2023, revenues from which would be used exclusively for transportation purposes that benefit the City of Fairfax.

 
Approved, unanimously to approve an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 90, Article II, Division 1, Section 90-33 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to classification and taxation of certain real property in the City of Fairfax used for or zoned for commercial and industrial purposes.  Such ordinance shall include a tax rate for such real property for calendar year 2023 of $0.125 per $100 of assessed value, revenues from which to be used exclusively for transportation purposes that benefit the City of Fairfax.
 
 

b.

Consideration of an ordinance amending Chapter 90 (Taxation), Article X (Old Town Service District Tax) of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to the additional tax imposed upon real property and improvements located within the Old Town Service District.

 
Approved, unanimously to approve an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 90, Article X, Section 90-422 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia, pertaining to the additional tax imposed upon real property and improvements located within the Old Town Service District for calendar year 2023 of $0.04 per $100.00 of assessed value.
 
 

c.

Consideration of an ordinance amending Chapter 102, Article II, Section 102-31 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to adjust the rates for wastewater service furnished by the City by 6.0%.

 
Approved, unanimously to approve an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 102, Article II, Section 102-31(b) of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to increase the rates for wastewater services by 6.0 percent.
 
 

d.

Consideration of an ordinance amending Chapter 102, Article V, Section 102-203 of the Code of the City of Fairfax, Virginia to set the rate for stormwater utility service furnished by the City at $30.52 per Billing Unit (BU).

 
Approved, unanimously to approve an ordinance amending and readopting Chapter 102, Article V, Section 102-203(b), setting the rate per billing unit to be used in calculatng the stormwater utility fee for the calendar year beginning January 1, 2023 at $30.52.
 
 

e.

Consideration of the City’s proposed FY 2024 Budget, Rates and Levies, FY 2024-2028 CIP, and consideration of an appropriation resolution for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater Utility, Cable, Old Town, Transportation tax and ARPA funds for FY 2024 based on the approved budget.

 
Approved, unanimously to adopt the General Fund, Capital Fund, Wastewater Fund, Stormwater Utility Fund, Cable Grant Fund, Old Town Service District Fund, Transit Fund, Transportation Tax Fund and ARPA Fund FY 2024 Budgets, as presented by the City Manager, together with the proposed FY 2024 Schedule of Rates and Levies, the FY 2024-2028 Capital Improvement Program, the FY 2024 health benefit contribution rates, a 3.5% merit increase effective January 1, 2024 for eligible general scale employees, a 2.0% market rate adjustment to general pay scales effective July 1, 2023, a 1% cost-of-living adjustment for public safety employees effective July 1, 2023, and the programmed annual step increase for public safety employees who were moved to the step system, as amended. The motion had previously been amended unanimously as follows: (i) to incorporate all changes included in the budget change document (Attachment I), (ii) to amend the Budget Change Document (Attachment I) to decrease the real estate revenues by $1,151,859 and increase appropriate fund balance by $1,151,859 as outlines in the Budget Change Document (Attachment I.C).

Approved, unanimously to set the real estate tax rate effective as of January 1, 2023 at $1.025 per one hundred dollars of assessed value, set the personal property and machinery and tools tax rate effective as of January 1, 2023 at $4.13 per one hundred dollars assessed valuation based upon the assessment ratio of one hundred percent of fair market value, and set the personal property tax rate effective as of January 1, 2023 at $.01 per one hundred dollars assessed valuation for those members of volunteer fire departments or volunteer rescue squads qualifying under the city code.

Approved, unanimously to approve the appropriation resolutions for the general, capital, wastewater, transit, stormwater utility, cable, old town, transportation tax and ARPA funds for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2023, reflecting all changes approved as part of the FY 2024 budget adoption, immediately prior to this action.
 
 
 
 4.  Adjournment.
