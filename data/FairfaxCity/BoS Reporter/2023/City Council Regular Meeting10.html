 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request of Lauren Heuser of Bond Vet VA LLC, applicant, for a Special Use Permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.3.1.B to allow an animal care facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District (ACOD), on the premises known as 9428 Fairfax Blvd and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel 48-3-02-011A.

 
Approved, unanimously that the City Council approve (without expiration) the request of Lauren Heuser of Bond Vet VA, LLC, applicant, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110.3.3.1.B to allow an animal care facility in the CR Commercial Retail Zoning District and Architectural Control Overlay District (ACOD), on the premises known as 9428 Fairfax Boulevard and more particularly described as tax map parcel 48-3-02-011A, subject to the following conditions:
  1. The operation of this establishment shall be consistent with Application, Statement of Support and Plans. The Zoning Administrator shall be enabled to approve minor changes.
  2. The applicant shall limit the hours of operation to seven days a week, 10:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m.
  3. The applicant shall incorporate sound mitigating measures into the design as shown on the submitted plans and details, for the proposed veterinarian hospital, to prevent noise levels, if any, from creating any adverse impact on adjacent commercial establishments or nearby residential properties.
  4. The applicant shall ensure all activities associated with the care and treatment of animals shall occur within the building.
  5. The applicant shall provide for proper disposal of medical waste and euthanized animals as required by local, state and federal regulations. All storage of waste shall be inside thefacility.
  6. The applicant shall monitor the shopping center premises to ensure that no animal waste accumulation occurs.
  7. All local, state and federal licenses and permits shall be maintained and kept current.
  8. The Special Use Permit shall be valid only for the applicant and shall not be transferrable to any other entity.
