                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         June 13, 2017 
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember DeMarco).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

a.

Presentation of a proclamation commemorating Fairfax County’s 275th Anniversary.

 

b.

Presentation of a donation to Britepaths from the National Hockey League playoff competition between Councilman Drummond for the Washington Capitals and Councilman Stehle for the Pittsburg Penguins.

 

c.

Presentation of the Chocolate Lovers Grant to Every1 Can Work d/b/a Cameron’s Coffee and Chocolates.

 

d.

Presentation of the City’s Audubon Wildlife Sanctuary Designation for Ashby Pond.

 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Introduction of an ordinance authorizing the City Manager to execute a Deed of Lease for a term beginning July 1, 2017 and ending June 30, 2019 by and between the City of Fairfax, Virginia, and Main Street Child Development Center, Inc., for approximately 7,364 square feet of space in the former Green Acres Elementary School, located at 4401 Sideburn Road.

 

b.

Consideration of a one-year extension to the Capital Funding Agreement with the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority.

 

c.

Consideration of a resolution setting the allocation percentage for Personal Property Tax Relief (PPTR) in the City of Fairfax for the 2017 tax year at 45.00%.

 

d.

Consideration of an award of contract to Ardent Co. LLC in the amount of $464,600 for the Draper Drive/Fairfax Blvd Signal Reconstruction Project.

 

e.

Consideration of a Task Order in the amount of $199,938 to Timmons Group for Engineering Services for the Government Center Parkway Extension Project.

 

f.

Consideration of an award of contract to Utilities Unlimited, LLC in the amount of $697,296.76 for the Burke Station Road drainage and pedestrian improvements project.

 

g.

Consideration of a Task Order to Rummel, Klepper & Kahl, LLP (RK&K) in the amount of $200,587 for Project Inspection and Oversight Consultant Services for Burke Station Road Storm Drainage and Sidewalk Project.

 

h.

Consideration of an award of contract to Lardner/Klein Landscape Architects and Frazier Associates to complete a rewrite of the City of Fairfax Design Guidelines and create an Old Town Improvement Plan.

 

i.

Consideration of a Task Order to Volkert, Inc., in an amount of $71,062, for project inspection and oversight consultant services for the Draper Drive Signal Reconstruction Project.

 
Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items.
 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public Comment and Council action on a resolution to proceed with or cancel the Northfax Road/University Drive Extension Project.

 
Approved, 4-3, a resolution to proceed with the University Drive Extension Project (UPC 109309). (Councilmembers Greenfield, Miller, and Schmidt opposed.)
 
 

b.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $584,200 for FY 2017 nondiscretionary expenditures.

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $584,200 for FY 2017 nondiscretionary expenditures.
 
 

c.

Public hearing and Council action on an appropriation resolution in the amount of $783,808 for Fiscal Year 2016-2017 Federal, State and other Grant Expenditures.

 
Approved, unanimously, an appropriation resolution in the amount of $783,808 for Fiscal Year 2016-2017 Federal, State and other Grant Expenditures.
 
 

d.

Public hearing and Council action on appropriation resolutions for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater, Cable, Old Town, and Transportation tax funds for the FY2018 budget year.

 
Approved, unanimously, appropriation resolutions for the General, Capital, Wastewater, Transit, Stormwater, Cable, Old Town, and Transportation tax funds for the FY2018 budget year.
