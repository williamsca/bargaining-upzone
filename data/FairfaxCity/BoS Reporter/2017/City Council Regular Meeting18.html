 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember Miller).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations.

 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of a Resolution to Endorse the City’s Submission to the Virginia Department of Transportation for FY 19 and 20 Revenue Sharing Funding.

 

Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda item.

 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings.

a.

Public hearing and Council action on the consideration of an application for federal Transportation Alternatives Program funding to construct the missing link of the Judicial Trail.  The Transportation Alternatives Program provides federal funding for projects that expand non-motorized travel choices. The conceptual design for the project will be available for review and there will be opportunity for public comment about the proposed funding application.

 

Approved, unanimously, to endorse the submission of a grant application to the Transportation Alternatives Program to fund the completion of the Judicial Drive Trail that is located between Hallman Drive and Judicial Drive.
 
 
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Consideration of an award of contract to Lemay Erickson Willcox Architects in an amount not to exceed $1,050,000 for architectural design and construction phase services for the Fire Station 33 Replacement Project.

 

Approved, unanimously, an award of a contract with Lemay Erickson Willcox Architects in an amount not to exceed $1,050,000 for architectural design and construction services for the Fire Station 33 Replacement Project and authorize the City Manager or his designee to execute the agreement provided that the agreement and any amendments are approved as to form by the City Attorney.
