                     COUNCIL ACTIONS OF
                         February 14, 2017 
 
   6:00 p.m. Mayoral Reception in the City Hall Atrium.
 
 1.  Call the regular meeting to order.
 
 2.  Invocation (Councilmember Schmidt).
 
 3.  Pledge of Allegiance.
 
 4.  Presentations and Proclamations. 
 
 5.  Presentations by the public on any item not subject to a public hearing.
 
 6.  Adoption of the agenda.
 
 7.  Consent agenda.

a.

Consideration of a resolution for the Reappropriation of Capital Project Funds to FY 2017 that were appropriated in FY 2016 and have not yet been spent as of June 30, 2016.

 

b.

Consideration of a special event application request by Fairfax Little League for their Annual Opening Day Ceremony/Parade.

 

c.

Consideration to appoint Lieutenant Ryan Hilt to Fire Inspector/Investigator.

 

d.

Introduction of a supplemental appropriation resolution for additional and essential bridge maintenance repairs in the amount of $110,285.75.

 

e.

Introduction of a supplemental appropriation resolution for the purchase of license plate reader equipment.

 

Approved, unanimously, the consent agenda items.

 
 
 
 8.  Public hearings. - None.
 
 9.  Items not requiring a public hearing.

a.

Public comment and City Council discussion and action regarding the date for a special election to fill the vacancy on the City Council resulting from the Mayoral special election; and consideration of the appointment of an individual to serve as an interim City Councilmember until a special election is held.

 
Approved, unanimously, a resolution authorizing and directing the City Attorney to file a petition for an order and writ of election to fill a vacancy on the City Council for the City of Fairfax. He further moved that, in the petition, the City Attorney request that the court set the date of September 12, 2017, for said special election.
 
 
 
 10.  Approval of minutes.

a.

January 24, 2016 regular meeting.

 
 
 11.  Convene a closed meeting to discuss disposition of publicly owned property, where discussion in an open meeting would adversely affect the bargaining position or negotiating strategy of the public body, appointments to Boards and Commissions and discussion of an interim appointment to the vacant City Council seat.
 
 12.  Actions out of Closed Meeting/Appointments to Boards and Commissions.
  • Reappointed Paul Cunningham as the Planning Commission Representative on the Board of Architectural Review for a one-year term extending through February 7, 2018.
  • Reappointed Janet Jaworski as the Planning Commission Representative on the Parks and Recreation Advisory Board for a one-year term extending through February 7, 2018.
  • Reappointed Sharon Clark Chang to the Commission on the Arts for a three-year term extending through February 11, 2020.
  • Reappointed Frank Franz as the City Representative on the Fairfax County Juvenile and Domestic Relations District Court Advisory Council to a two-year term extending through March 10, 2019.
  • Reappointed Jocelyn Rappaport as the City Representative on the Fairfax Area Long Term Care Coordinating Council for a three-year term extending through February 22, 2020.
  • Confirmed the election of Joel Hendelman as the Retiree Representative, as elected by the current retirees, to The Retirement Plan Administrative Committee, for a three-year term commencing on March 1, 2017 through February 28, 2020.
