a.

Public hearing and Council action on a request from Khosrow Rezaeipour, applicant, by Keith C. Martin, Agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow dancing and entertainment areas as accessory uses to a restaurant (Epicure Café) in the commercial retail zoning district on the premises known as 11104 Lee Highway and more particularly described as Tax Map Parcel(s) 57-1-02-033.

 

Approved, unanimously, the request of Khosrow Rezaeipour, applicant, by Keith C. Martin, agent, for a special use permit pursuant to City Code Section 110-3.5.5 to allow dancing and entertainment areas as accessory uses to a restaurant (Epicure Café) in the commercial retail zoning district on the premises known as 11104 Lee Highway and more particularly described as tax map parcel(s) 57-1-02-033, subject to the following conditions:

  1. Dancing and live entertainment shall be limited to the hours of 8:00 p.m. to 1:00 a.m., every day, accessory to the restaurant use.
  2. Dancing and entertainment activities shall be restricted to the 288 square foot area depicted on the proposed floor plan.
  3. The applicant shall maintain a parking agreement with the property owner/management guaranteeing parking spaces available to Epicure Café from the hours of 8:00 pm to 1:00 am.
  4. The special use permit shall expire on May 23, 2018, however, the special use permit shall automatically renew for an additional two-year period if staff is satisfied that there have not  been significant adverse events that have occurred at the restaurant in the first year of operation under this special use permit.
  5. Annually, following the date of approval of this special use permit, the applicant shall report to the Zoning Administrator on the events that have occurred in the restaurant and all known violations and complaints.
