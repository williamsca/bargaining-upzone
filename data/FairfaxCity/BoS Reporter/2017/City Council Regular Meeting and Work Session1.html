a.

Consideration of a resolution to proceed with preliminary engineering for the University Drive Extension Project.

 

Approved, 5-1, a resolution to proceed with 30% preliminary engineering for the University Drive Extension Project (UPC 109309). Councilmember Miller further moved to approve a resolution to proceed with preliminary engineering including evaluation of potential street design modifications for the University Drive Extension Project (UPC 109309) and:

            Whereas, the City has developed a conceptual plan for the University Drive Extension, which was submitted to VDOT as part of the Smart Scale (previously HB2) funding application, which includes construction of a new roadway between Fairfax Boulevard and Eaton Place north of University Drive; and

            Whereas, the property owners in the project area have been notified of the project and invited to comment on it; and

            Whereas, the estimated project costs including right of way/easement acquisition and construction will be approximately $10,000,000; and

            Whereas, the City applied for and received Smart Scale funds from the Virginia Department of Transportation for the project; and

            Now therefore, be it resolved by the City Council of the City of Fairfax, Virginia that the City Council approves the recommendation of staff to proceed with the preliminary engineering including evaluation of potential street design modifications for the University Drive Extension (UPC 109309). (Councilmember Schmidt opposed.)

 

Approved, unanimously, that the City of Fairfax initiate a traffic study on University Drive between Fairfax Boulevard and Kenmore Drive and direct staff to work with residents as soon as possible pursuant to the City’s Traffic Calming Policy.
